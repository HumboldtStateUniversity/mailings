A Message from Humboldt State University

February 18, 2015

New campus announcement on myHumboldt

Dear Campus Employee,

An important work-related message has been posted to the Campus Announcements pagelet in myHumboldt. Please review it by logging in to myHumboldt.

The campus is expanding the use of myHumboldt this semester, using it to regularly post important messages for employees. This will make messages easily accessible and searchable, while reducing the number of all-campus emails. These emails about new Campus Announcement postings are part of the transition, and will only be sent until Feb. 20.

myHumboldt has a number of resources, including the messaging center. The messaging center includes the Campus Announcements pagelet, which shows the most recent all-campus messages, as well as pagelets with messages related to your Division and College/Unit.

In any of the message pagelets, you can scroll down to a link to read more messages or subscribe to receive messages by email. On each message, you may get a printable version. To search archived messages, link to a full message, click on one of the campus divisions/units in the left-hand navigation, and then enter search terms at the top of the page.
