Commencement 2021

Hello 2021 Graduates!

We know you’re eager to hear what’s happening with Commencement this year.

We’re working to provide some kind of regional in-person celebrations throughout the state. Keep an eye out for more information about dates and locations in the next few weeks.

Because the situation is changing regularly, we’re also planning for a virtual ceremony in case we need to pivot from any in-person plans. There will be a single registration that covers both the planned in-person events and, if necessary, the virtual ceremony.

Regardless of what the ceremony looks like, you’ll be invited to submit a photo of yourself to commemorate your big moment. Many of you will want to wear your cap and gown, and caps, rental gowns, tassels, hoods, and more are now available to order on the Humboldt State Herff Jones website. Orders will be shipped directly to you, and return shipping for the rental gowns is included in the shipping fee of $12.95.

The safety of our Lumberjack community and their families is a priority, and our efforts will be informed by public health and university guidelines and protocols. All plans are dependent on approval by state and county public health officials and are subject to change based on the status of the pandemic.

Remember, when it’s safe to hold a traditional Commencement ceremony at HSU again, all alumni—whether you graduate this year, last year, or several years ago—are welcome to return for Commencement in Redwood Bowl.

We're excited to celebrate with you, and appreciate your patience and feedback as we work to find creative and safe ways to celebrate your success.


Sincerely,
