Commencement 2016 Saturday, May 14 | Redwood Bowl

Commencement is Just Around the Corner!

Dear Student,

As you prepare for finals and make plans for Commencement, here are some tips to help you prepare for the big day:

  • Pick up your pre-printed name card next Thursday, May 12 or Friday, May 13 from 8 a.m. to 4 p.m. in the front lobby of the Student Business Services Building. Be prepared to give your HSU ID# to the people passing them out.
  • Arrive in your cap and gown WITH your name card at the West Gym no later than:
    • 7:45 a.m.— College of Arts, Humanities & Social Sciences
    • 11:15 a.m.— College of Natural Resources & Sciences
    • 2:45 p.m.— College of Professional Studies
  • If you or any of your guests need disability-related accommodations, contact the Student Disability Resource Center at (707) 826-4678 or sdrc@humboldt.edu.
  • We will be live-streaming the Commencement ceremonies and there will be a link on the HSU homepage starting Thursday, May 12. In addition, limited seating is available to view the live stream in Room 166 of the Behavioral & Social Sciences Building and Van Duzer Theatre.
  • Bring a water bottle to the ceremony—stations will be set up around the bowl to refill.
  • Check the weather forecast and come prepared. Here is my favorite weather website.

We’re looking forward to celebrating your accomplishments alongside your family and friends. Good luck with finals!

Sincerely,

Kristen Gould
P.S. The name cards are really important! Please re-read the first bullet ;-)
