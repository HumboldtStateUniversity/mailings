Fall Commencement 2021
Humboldt State University is excited to announce that it will host the first-ever Fall Commencement ceremony to recognize the achievements of HSUâ€™s Fall 2021 graduates. There will be a single ceremony for graduates in all Colleges on Friday, Dec. 17 at 3 p.m. in Lumberjack Arena.
The ceremony will be livestreamed, and a link to the livestream will be available on the Commencement website and HSU homepage the day of the ceremony.
  • Registration: Graduates MUST register by Nov. 8 to participate in Commencement. This is in addition to officially applying for graduation. More information on how to register will be sent to eligible students within the next week.
  • Guests: Guest seating will be limited based on the number of graduates attending. The number of tickets that will be available to each graduate will be shared when guest registration opens in late November.
  • Health Requirements: Masking will be required for everyone in attendance, and all HSU COVID-19 safety protocols will remain in place. More details will be shared closer to the event if health requirements modify due to policy driven by the CSU, state and public health.
  • Faculty: Faculty are encouraged to be part of the celebrations and support students at Commencement. If needed, you may rent regalia for the ceremony through Herff Jones. Details about how to participate in the Commencement ceremony will be coming soon.
  • Staff: The number of support staff working Commencement will be limited. Anyone interested in helping to support Commencement should talk to their supervisor and contact grad@humboldt.edu.
  • Watch Commencement: Ceremonies will be livestreamed so that families, friends, and the campus community can watch and help graduates celebrate their big moment. There will be a link on humboldt.edu the days of the events. A video recording will be available online after the ceremony, and can be downloaded for free from the commencement.humboldt.edu in the weeks following.
  • Regalia and Memorabilia: Caps, rental gowns, tassels, hoods, and more are now available to order on the Humboldt State Herff Jones website. If you have any questions regarding regalia orders, please contact the HSU Bookstore at (707) 826-3741.
  • Photos: GradImages photographers will be on hand to capture official graduation portraits. After the ceremony, students will be sent a link to preview their images, and there is no obligation to purchase photos.
The safety of our Lumberjack community and their families is a priority. All plans are developed utilizing guidelines from state and county public health offices, and are subject to change based on the status of the pandemic.
For questions about Commencement ceremonies or student registration, contact Commencement coordinators Kristen Gould and Stephanie Lane at grad@humboldt.edu.
