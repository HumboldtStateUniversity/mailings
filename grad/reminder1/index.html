Commencement 2016 Saturday, May 14 | Redwood Bowl

Deadline to Register for Commencement is April 27

Dear Student,

Graduation is almost here! If you plan to walk in the Commencement ceremonies on May 14, here are a few things you need to do.

1. Register by April 27, 2016 if you haven’t already done it. The ceremonies are organized by College and there are three to choose from. If you are a double major, you can choose to walk in more than one ceremony. Don’t forget to fill out the phonetic spelling of your name so we know how to pronounce it on stage.

2. If you missed the deadline to pre-order your cap and gown from the HSU Bookstore, additional rental and keeper gowns will be available in the store starting April 27. Sizes are limited, so get in early!

If you have questions about Commencement, contact us at grad@humboldt.edu or 707.826.3321.

Sincerely,
