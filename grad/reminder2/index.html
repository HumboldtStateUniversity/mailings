Commencement 2016 Saturday, May 14 | Redwood Bowl

Tips for Commencement

Dear Student,

Right now, you probably have a million things on your mind: classes, finals, summer, and life beyond college. To help make Commencement as smooth as possible for you, here are a few tips and reminders:

  • Bring your cap, gown, tassel, plus other regalia you’d like to wear to the ceremony. If you pre-ordered regalia from the HSU Bookstore, it is available for pick up until Friday at 6 pm. If you missed the deadline to pre-order your cap and gown from the HSU Bookstore, additional rental and keeper gowns are available in the store as well. Sizes are limited, so get in early!
  • Pick up your pre-printed name card on May 12 or 13 from 8 a.m. to 4 p.m. at the Admissions Visitor Center in the front lobby of the Student Business Services Building. Be prepared to give your HSU ID# to the person giving them out.
  • Plan to stay for the entire ceremony, out of respect for your classmates and your families. Allow enough time after the end of the Commencement ceremony to get to restaurants and other activities off campus. Ceremonies last between 2 and 2.5 hours, so it's strongly recommended that you make restaurant reservations a minimum of 3.5 hours after the start of the ceremony.
  • Bring a water bottle—stations will be set up around the bowl to refill.
  • Commencement will happen rain or shine, so be prepared for any weather.

The commencement website has more information. Also, as an alumni-to-be, you might be interested in Forever Humboldt. This association is a great way to stay connected to students, faculty, staff, or alumni, and membership is free.

If you have questions, feel free to contact the Commencement Office at grad@humboldt.edu or call 707.826.3321.

Good luck with the rest of your semester!

Sincerely,

Kristen Gould
