Commencement 2017 Saturday, May 13 | Redwood Bowl

Hello everyone!

As the dust has settled from another successful Commencement, we want to express sincere appreciation for the hard work you all put into making the day a huge success!

Even though it rained as we predicted, it wasn't enough to deter families from watching their students walk across the stage. Those of you working out in the rain hung in there, and when there were last minute problems to be solved, everyone pulled together and we made it a great day.

It takes a lot of people behind the scenes to make three large Commencement ceremonies happen smoothly. There were almost 400 people who worked on Saturday, not to mention the many who helped prepare in advance. But seeing the students celebrate this huge milestone with their proud families helps us all understand why we're doing the jobs we're doing here at HSU. Your willingness to help and can-do-it attitude is what makes working with this awesome staff and student team so rewarding for us both.

Be sure to check out some fun Commencement photos here.

So THANK YOU again so much for helping out this year! We hope to see you back again in 2018.

Sincerely,

Kristen Gould & Stephanie Lane
