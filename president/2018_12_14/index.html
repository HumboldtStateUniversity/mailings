December 14, 2018
Full Day off December 24

In celebration of the holiday season, Governor Brown has authorized a half-day informal time off on Monday, December 24. In addition, I am approving a half-day administrative leave that day, and many campus offices will not be operational for the entire day.

The campus will need a limited number of essential personnel to work that day. Your appropriate administrator will notify you if you fall into that category. Those assigned to work on December. 24 will be able to make arrangements for an informal day off before June 30, 2019.

I hope this additional time will help support the safety of your travel and planning for the holidays, and that the break will provide time for you to relax and connect with family and friends. I look forward to working with you the new year!

With best wishes for the holidays,

Lisa A. Rossbacher, Ph.D.
President
