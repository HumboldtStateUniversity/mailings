Dear Campus Community,

Please join me in congratulating HSU Professor Bernadette Cheyne, who has been appointed as Faculty Trustee on the CSU Board of Trustees

Bernadette is a professor in Theatre, Film & Dance, where she regularly directs productions. She has many years of governance experience, including a variety of leadership positions on our Academic Senate and on the CSU Statewide Senate.

I believe Bernadette will bring an important perspective to the Board of Trustees. At the same time, the experience will provide her a broader understanding of the issues facing the system and its 23 campuses.

It's worth noting that another member of the Board is closely affiliated with Humboldt. Student Trustee Steven Dixon is a recent HSU graduate who is currently pursuing his master's degree at Sacramento State.

Bernadette, I wish you all the best in this new role. I am proud that you are serving as CSU's Faculty Trustee, and I know your faculty colleagues and other members of our campus community are proud as well.

Sincerely,


Rollin C. Richmond
President
