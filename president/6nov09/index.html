Dear Students and Colleagues,

Recently, there has been a speaker on the quad that has disturbed many individuals by the content of his messages. Humboldt State University values the Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Queer and Intersex (LGBTQI) community and remains committed to the expression of diverse viewpoints and creating a respectful educational environment. While we regret the hateful speech on the quad, as a public institution and state agency we cannot prohibit such speech except in rare cases where the statements incite violence or constitute harassment. It is important to uphold the right of individuals to speak, even when the speech is distasteful or worse.

An effective response to hate speech is action that helps to affirm and create a more just world. We commend the students, staff and faculty for taking this as an educational opportunity to create a positive space of affirmation of all members of our community.

Sincerely,
