University Community Holiday Celebration, December 4

Dear colleagues and friends:

As we move toward the end of the fall semester here at Humboldt State, I want to thank you all for the hard work you do on behalf of our university and, especially, our students. For me, the last few months have been an amazing introduction to this wonderful institution and community, and I am grateful for the mission, the people, and the spirit of HSU.

An important tradition is our annual employee gathering to celebrate the season, our successes, and the achievements of our students. I hope you will join the party on Thursday, December 4, any time between 3:30 and 5:30 p.m., in the Kate Buchanan Room. We will have appetizers, desserts, beverages, and an opportunity to share our sense of community. I plan to make a few brief remarks at about 4:30 p.m.

Please consider bringing canned or boxed food items to donate to our local food bank. Donation bins will be available.

I look forward to seeing you there!

With best wishes,

Lisa A. Rossbacher, Ph.D.
President
