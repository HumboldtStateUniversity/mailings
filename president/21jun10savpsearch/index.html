Dear Colleagues:

We have begun the process of a national search for a new Vice President of Enrollment Management and Student Affairs.

The position announcement will be posted in early July, and the search committee will convene in August following convocation. The committee's goal is to hold interviews on campus by late fall.

There will be many opportunities for the campus community and relevant groups to meet with the finalists. A candidate feedback form will be available so that anyone may send comments to the search committee. All of this input will be considered carefully prior to making a final decision.

This is a critical position for Humboldt State and it is important that we attract good candidates. Campus participation in the search process will help make the best decision for the University.

During the search, Dr. Edward "Buzz" Webb will serve as interim vice president. He will begin his appointment in the first week of July. Buzz is no stranger to many of you since he served Humboldt State in the capacity of Dean for Student Services and then Vice President of Student Affairs for a total of 30 years. Please join me in welcoming him back to campus.
