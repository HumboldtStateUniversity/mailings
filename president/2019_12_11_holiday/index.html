Monday, Dec. 16
4–6 p.m.
Kate Buchanan Room
A tradition on campus is our annual employee gathering to celebrate the season, our achievements, and the success of our students. I hope you will join me on Monday, December 16, anytime between 4 to 6 p.m. in the Kate Buchanan Room.
We will have a variety of appetizers, desserts, and music. Two complimentary drink tickets will be given at the door for each attendee; beverage service will begin at 4 p.m. I plan to make a few brief remarks around 4:30 p.m.
Please bring an item for our on-campus food bank, Oh SNAP! The items that they need most are canned meat, soups, stews, chili, peanut butter, beans, pasta, rice, cereal, oatmeal, and canned fruit. Your sharing with others in this season will be greatly appreciated.
I look forward to seeing you there!
With best wishes,
Tom Jackson, Jr., Ed.D.
President
