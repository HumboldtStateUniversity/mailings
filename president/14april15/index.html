April 14, 2015
Draft of Strategic Plan Available – Open Forum April 23

Dear University Community,

The Strategic Plan is one step closer to being finalized. The Steering Committee’s draft is now online for review here.

We have scheduled an open forum where you can provide feedback about the draft plan from 1 to 3 p.m. Thursday, April 23 in the Kate Buchanan Room. You can also provide feedback by email at strategic@humboldt.edu through the end of the day on April 23.

The Steering Committee will create a new version of the plan based on your input and then present it to the University Senate for final approval at its May 5 meeting.

Your voice is an invaluable part of this process, so please make sure to share your ideas!

Sincerely,

Lisa A. Rossbacher, Ph.D., President
Noah Zerbe, Ph.D., Chair, University Senate and Chair, Department of Politics
Co-Chairs, HSU Strategic Planning Steering Committee
