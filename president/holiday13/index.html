President's Holiday Message

November 15, 2013
 

Friends and Colleagues,

The holiday season is just about here, and we would once again like to recognize your good work and enjoy each other's company with a campus-wide party.

Please plan to join us any time between 3:30 and 5:30 p.m. on Thursday, December 5, in the Kate Buchanan Room.

The faculty and staff holiday party is an occasion to thank each other for helping our students succeed and to celebrate our shared mission. Appetizers, desserts and beverages will be provided.

I look forward to seeing you all there!

Sincerely,

Rollin C. Richmond
President
