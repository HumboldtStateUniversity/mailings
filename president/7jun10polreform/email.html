Dear Colleagues and Students,

California's political system is, quite frankly, a mess, and its educational systems are suffering as a consequence.

One source of hope for change is a bipartisan group called California Forward. This organization is based in Sacramento and is working to help restructure the dysfunctional system of governance in California.

I invite you to attend a special campus presentation by the Executive Director of California Forward, James Mayer, and his colleague, Steve Reed, from 8:30–9:30 a.m. on Tuesday, June 15 in Goodwin Forum. They will talk about some of the challenges facing California and explain the objectives of their organization.

I hope that you will be able to attend to learn what California Forward is doing and what you can do to help. You can find out more about California Forward by going to www.caforward.org.

Sincerely,

Rollin C. Richmond
President
