April 21, 2015
Reminder – Strategic Plan Open Forums April 22 and 23

Dear University Community,

HSU’s Strategic Plan is one step closer to being finalized. The Steering Committee’s draft is online (PDF) for review.

The Strategic Planning Steering Committee invites you to share feedback in two open forums this week:

  • Wednesday, April 22, 9-11 a.m., Great Hall
  • Thursday, April 23, 1-3 p.m., Kate Buchanan Room

You can also provide feedback by email at strategic@humboldt.edu through the end of the day on April 23.

The Steering Committee will create a new version of the plan based on your input and then present it to the University Senate for final approval at its May 5 meeting. Your voice is an invaluable part of this process, so please make sure to share your ideas!

Additional information is available on the strategic plan website.
