April 10, 2015
Please complete the survey
for Great Colleges to Work For

Dear Colleagues:

You recently received an e-mail from The Chronicle of Higher Education's 2015 Great Colleges to Work For program asking you to participate in a confidential employee survey. For the first time in many years, Humboldt State is participating in this survey, and your responses are critical to understanding ways in which we are successful and areas in which we need some work.

If you haven’t already done so, I would greatly appreciate it if you could take a few minutes to participate in this important survey. The deadline is Monday, April 13 at 9 p.m. PDT.

In my experience, the results of this survey are an invaluable measure of morale, employee satisfaction, and working conditions. The information that will be provided to campus will help us improve the ways in which we work together. My goal in wanting to participate is not to be recognized as a “Great College to Work For” (although that would be wonderful), but to establish a baseline and identify ways we can improve the working environment on campus.

With best wishes,

Lisa A. Rossbacher, Ph.D.,
President
