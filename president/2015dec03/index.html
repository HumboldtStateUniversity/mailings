December 3, 2015
University Community Holiday Celebration
December 11

Dear colleagues and friends:

As we approach the end of the fall semester here at Humboldt State, I want to thank you all for your hard work on behalf of our University and, especially, our students. This semester has gone incredibly fast, and I continue to be grateful for the mission, the people, and the spirit of HSU.

An enduring tradition is our annual employee gathering to celebrate the season, our successes, and the achievements of our students. I hope you will join the party on Friday, December 11, any time between 3:30 and 5:30 p.m. in the Kate Buchanan Room. We will have appetizers, desserts, non-alcoholic beverages, and a cash bar. There will also be a craft fair, which has been organized by Staff Council. I plan to make a few brief remarks at about 4:30 p.m.

Please bring non-perishable canned or boxed food items to donate to our local food bank. Your willingness to share with others in this season will be greatly appreciated.

I look forward to seeing you there!

With best wishes,

Lisa A. Rossbacher, Ph.D.
President
