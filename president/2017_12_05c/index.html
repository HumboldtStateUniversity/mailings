December 5, 2017
Thank You for Helping HSU Preserve Football

Dear Lumberjack Boosters,

Earlier today, I met with student-athletes, coaches, and others to share the good news that Humboldt State University will continue fielding our football team. Football will remain a part of our Division II Intercollegiate Athletics program, along with all of our other sports.

It was a real joy to tell the players what I am writing to tell you: Today is a good day for Lumberjack Football!

Of course, you made this possible. Your tremendous outpouring of support during the fund drive was inspiring, especially because you accomplished what you did in such a short period of time.

Jim Redd, who helped lead the fund drive effort along with Ceva Courtemanche, joined me for today’s announcement. I can report that Jim represented you extremely well, and he assured the players and coaches that they have a large community of boosters who care about them. The emotion on the faces of the players made it clear they understood.

Many of you wanted news of this decision earlier, and I apologize for that not being possible. We were working very hard to achieve a positive outcome for football. In this case, that meant taking extra time, having additional conversations, and being flexible. I also want to acknowledge that the last couple months have been challenging, as we shared frustrations and had differing ideas about the right approach. I genuinely hope we can move beyond that, with a focus on our shared commitment to HSU and our students.

Going forward, it will be important that we create a smooth and transparent process for seeking commitments and collecting payments. The goal remains raising $500,000 per year for each of the next five years, much of which has already been promised. We will seek to have cash on hand each year by January 31, which will allow collecting funds during the football season and will give the University time to ensure we are staying on track. I have asked Jim Redd to involve others who led the fund drive effort and to work closely with our HSU Advancement Foundation to clarify details.

For now, I want to share with you that I have fulfilled my personal commitment for this year to the Support Your Jacks fund drive. I encourage each of you to fulfill your commitments now as well.

Remember the deadline for fulfilling commitments is January 31 each year. You may do so online through the “Support Your Jacks” link at hsujacks.com or by check (checks payable to "HSU Athletics," designated to Support Your Jacks on the memo line and mailed to: Gift Processing Center, SBS 285, Humboldt State University, 1 Harpst Street, Arcata CA 95521)

Once again, thank you for stepping up on behalf of our student-athletes. We are proud of our players, and we are proud that you have chosen to support them and Humboldt State University.

Sincerely,

Lisa A. Rossbacher, Ph.D.
President
