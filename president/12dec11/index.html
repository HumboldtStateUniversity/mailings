President's Holiday Message

December 12, 2011
 

Happy Holidays

Friends and Colleagues,

Thank you to everyone who participated in our new Faculty and Staff Holiday Party last week, and a special thanks to those who helped plan and staff it. I understand it was a great success. I wish I could have joined you, but it was important for me to attend an Executive Council meeting in Long Beach during these uncertain financial times.

It is hard to believe, but final exams are getting underway today and another semester is coming to a close.

I want to extend to you my best wishes for a happy holiday season. I hope you are able to enjoy the time off for rest and rejuvenation, as well as the opportunity to spend extra time with family and friends. You deserve it.

Sometime over the next few weeks, you may want to spend some reflecting on the many ways in which you make a difference for our students, and congratulate yourself. Your efforts - large and small, campus-wide and one-to-one - all contribute to our students' academic and social development. I believe that experience, of making a lasting and positive difference, is one of the great benefits of working in higher education.

Please have a safe and enjoyable holiday season. I look forward to continuing our work together next semester.

Sincerely,

Rollin C. Richmond
President
