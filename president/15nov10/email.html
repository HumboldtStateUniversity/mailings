Colleagues,

Last spring, the Cabinet for Institutional Change released a report that offered specific recommendations for change at our university. To oversee and report to the campus community the progress of these recommendations, the Provost and I established the Change Steering Committee.

Because there were many recommendations in the Cabinet's report, the Change Steering Committee facilitated a meeting with leaders from the faculty, students, staff, and administration that identified a number of priorities to focus on this academic year. The committee has just established a website that contains progress reports on each of these priorities. These will be updated twice a semester.

I hope you will take the opportunity to visit this website from time to time and forward your comments and suggestions to the Change Steering Committee. I would like to thank the members of this committee, led by Dr. John Meyer, and also the individuals around campus who are advancing each of these initiatives.


Rollin C. Richmond
President
