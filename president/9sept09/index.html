Dear Students and Colleagues:

The Associated Students Council recently passed a resolution that partially reads as follows:

"The Humboldt State University Associated Students Government hereby support and promote the observance of 60 seconds of silence to take place at exactly 8:46 AM on Friday, September 11, 2009 by all students, faculty, and staff across campus, wherever they happen to be at that moment, in recognition and remembrance of those who lost their lives in the 9/11 attacks on American soil."

I encourage each of you to consider following the recommendation of our student leaders and join me in a minute of silence for those who died on September 11, 2001.

Sincerely,
Rollin C. Richmond
President
