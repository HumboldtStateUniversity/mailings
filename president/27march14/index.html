Dear Students,

This country, our state, the CSU, and HSU are going through a transition because of political and institutional change. We need our young people, who will shortly take control of the politics of the state and nation, to become educated and experienced. Please vote in the upcoming Associated Students election and consider becoming a candidate yourself.

We have long had many opportunities for students to become engaged and to learn how to be effective leaders, many of these opportunities stem from being a leader in A.S. For more information about what is available, please go to: humboldt.edu/associatedstudents/

Real change is valued at HSU, and in order to be a part of a positive change you need to be involved. The best way to get your voice heard is to speak up, and Associated Students is one of the best resources on campus to do that.

A.S. has substantial power to make a difference in your lives as students, with an annual budget of just over $1.5 million, and student representatives who sit on powerful campus committees like the University Senate and Executive Committee. If you have concerns about how the campus is administered or the costs based around tuition, be sure you elect students who will represent those interests well. Or better yet, run yourself and make a positive change!

Nomination materials for the A.S. Council are now currently available at the A.S. Office located in the University Center, South Lounge. The deadline for completed application materials is Monday, April 7, 2014 at 5 p.m.

Sincerely,

Rollin C. Richmond
