May 1, 2015
New Draft of Strategic Plan

Dear Colleagues,

A revised draft of the Strategic Plan has been updated with feedback from previous forums and email comments, and this new version is now online.

This draft will be sent to the University Senate for its review on Tuesday, May 5. The HSU community will have a final opportunity to provide feedback on the Strategic Plan until 5 p.m. that day.

After that, one more set of revisions will be made based on any new input. The final version will be presented to the Senate for its consideration on Tuesday, May 12.

Thank you for the many insightful comments received throughout this planning process. We look forward to finalizing the Strategic Plan and beginning its implementation.

Sincerely,

Lisa A. Rossbacher, Ph.D., President
Noah Zerbe, Ph.D., Chair, University Senate and Chair, Department of Politics
Co-Chairs, HSU Strategic Planning Steering Committee
