Dear Lumberjacks,
With the semester coming to a close, I find myself filled with gratitude for this community and all that we’ve achieved together. The energy, creativity, and care you bring to Cal Poly Humboldt have made this a truly special place to serve, and the moments we’ve shared will stay with me for years to come.
This community has shown me the very best of what higher education can be—collaborative, compassionate, and deeply connected to the people and place we call home. Watching our students grow, our programs flourish, and our shared vision for the future take shape has been an experience I’ll always carry with me.
While this marks the end of my time at Humboldt, it brings me great joy to welcome Dr. Carvajal, who will continue to lead this remarkable university with the same dedication and heart that define our campus. Please join me in offering him the same kindness and partnership that made my time here so meaningful.
To our graduates, congratulations on reaching this incredible milestone! Your hard work and determination embody what it means to be a Lumberjack, and I can’t wait to see where your journeys take you next. To the entire campus community, thank you for allowing me to be part of the Cal Poly Humboldt family. Serving as your interim president has been one of the greatest honors of my career.
Wishing you peace, rest, and joy throughout the holiday season.
With deep appreciation and warm wishes,
Michael E. Spagna
Interim President
