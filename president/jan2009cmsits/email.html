Colleagues,

I am pleased to inform you that we are merging Common Management Systems (CMS) into Information Technology Services (ITS). This unit will be managed by Anna Kircher, HSU's chief information officer, and will continue to be part of Academic Affairs. The change will be effective Feb. 1.

Information technology and CMS are vitally important to our operations. Through this change, I am confident that our staff in these areas will be able to operate even more effectively and efficiently. They will be better able to share expertise and resources, leading to enhanced service to the campus community. In the short term, the unit will be focused on integrating the staff while ensuring that campus users do not experience any disruptions of service.

I am appreciative of efforts by numerous campus units and individuals to make this change happen.

Rollin Richmond
President
