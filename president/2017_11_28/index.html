November 28, 2017
President’s Message: Campus leadership this week

Dear Students and Colleagues:

At the beginning of the fall break, I was looking forward to celebrating my mother’s 93rd birthday today. I am deeply grateful for the opportunity to spend some time with her over a few days, before she passed away two nights ago, on the day before her birthday. For me, her loss has highlighted the importance of valuing every moment we have with friends, family, and loved ones.

I will be staying in Virginia for a few extra days for the funeral service, and will be away from campus for the rest of this week.

The University is in good hands. Craig Wruck, Vice President for University Advancement, served as acting president yesterday; Doug Dawes, Vice President for Administrative Affairs, will serve in this role on Tuesday and Wednesday; and Alex Enyedi, Provost and Vice President for Academic Affairs, will be acting president on Thursday and Friday.

Please give a hug or a kind word to someone you care about today.

Warmly,

Lisa A. Rossbacher, Ph.D.
President
