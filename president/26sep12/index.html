Colleagues:

Change is upon us. On Monday, October 8, from 10-11 am in Goodwin Forum, we will welcome our new Vice President of Administration and Finance, Joyce Lopes. Joyce, a CPA, comes to us after several years of service as the Chief Business Officer for the Western Placer Unified School District in Lincoln, California. She has also worked in the CSU as the Director of Grants and Contract Administration for CSU Sacramento. Joyce has an undergraduate degree from the University of Maryland and an MBA from the University of Colorado.

This will also be an opportunity to thank Vice President Burt Nordstrom for his many years of service to HSU but especially his excellent work as our Vice President for Administrative Affairs over the last several years.

Please join me to say thanks to a friend and colleague and wish him a well-deserved retirement and welcome to a new friend and colleague who will help us move forward.



Rollin C. Richmond
President
