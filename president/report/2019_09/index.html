President's Report
September
2019
It's great to have the Fall 2019 semester underway. During my first two months, I've gotten to meet a wide range of faculty and staff, students, alumni, and community members. Thank you for the warm welcome!
This is my first President's Report, and my goal is to send one monthly.
You'll keep hearing me say this: I'm motivated by student success. I want our students to have a positive, meaningful educational experience. That means mentoring, coaching, and connecting with students. It also means supporting them with the various resources we have on campus, and in the community, so they can enjoy success at Humboldt. Let's celebrate our students' accomplishments as well as those of the whole campus.

— President Jackson

Thank You, Coach Meiggs!

After 25 seasons as head coach of the Humboldt State women's rowing team, Robin Meiggs is retiring. Coach Meiggs has enjoyed a remarkable career at Humboldt State, and is recognized as one of the University's most successful coaches. She helped grow the Rowing program from a club sport into a Division II powerhouse. The team has won two national championships and its student-athletes have excelled academically.

Give to the Student Adversity Fund and Double Your Impact

Many students face unexpected challenges that may derail their education. The Student Adversity Fund assists current Humboldt students who have an emergency need that could force them to drop out of school. This year all gifts to the Student Adversity Fund will be doubled up to $50,000 in donations.

New Students Engage with Humbot

HSU's new text-based chatbot, called HumBot, introduced itself via text message to first-time freshmen and new transfer students the first week of classes. The free service, available to answer questions at any time, is another resource to help students navigate HSU life and feel more connected to the University and, in turn, increase retention.
