HSU’s Future: Weigh in on what matters most

Dear Colleagues,

Envisioning where HSU needs to be in five years is an exciting challenge, and it can’t be done without you.

So please weigh in and help plan HSU’s future. You may be invited to take a survey, and you’ll have a chance to participate in a brainstorming workshop this Friday, Feb. 13.

Both the survey and the workshop will focus on the university’s key priorities:

  • Making sure the student academic experience is successful
  • Supporting a diverse and inclusive campus community
  • Ensuring financial resources to fulfill the university’s mission

The email survey, which will include open-ended questions, is voluntary. Faculty, staff, and students are encouraged to attend the workshop. Don’t forget to register, and feel free to take a look at the questions participants will address at the workshop.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,

Lisa A. Rossbacher, Ph.D., President
Noah Zerbe, Ph.D., Chair, University Senate and Chair, Department of Politics
Co-Chairs, HSU Strategic Planning Steering Committee
