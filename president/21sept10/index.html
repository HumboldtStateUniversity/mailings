Dear Students and Colleagues:

I am pleased to announce that we have two new University Ombudspersons who will serve our campus during the upcoming year, Professors Nancy Hurlbut and Steve Steinberg.

The University Ombudspersons provide confidential, informal mediation and conflict resolution services, to the extent permitted by applicable law, to students, faculty and staff, with a primary focus on student concerns.

The Ombudspersons serve as a resource to promote a positive campus climate and environment by facilitating problem resolution through listening, providing information, making referrals to other campus resources and identifying options and alternatives.

For more information about the University Ombudspersons, including their contact information and how they may be of assistance, please visit: http://www.humboldt.edu/ombuds/.

Thank you, Nancy and Steve, for your service on our behalf.

I wish you all the best,
