University Community Holiday Celebration,
December 14

Dear colleagues and friends:

As we approach the end of the fall semester, I want to thank you all for your hard work on behalf of Humboldt State University and, especially, our students. This semester has gone incredibly fast, and I am more grateful than ever for the mission, the people, and the spirit of HSU.

An important tradition is our annual employee gathering to celebrate the season, our successes, and the achievements of our students. I hope you will join the celebration on Wednesday, December 14, any time between 4:30 and 6:30 p.m. in the Kate Buchanan Room. We will have appetizers, desserts, and music. One complimentary drink ticket will be provided at the door for each attendee; service will begin at 5:00 p.m. I plan to make a few brief remarks at about 5:15 p.m.

Please bring non-perishable canned or boxed food items to donate to our on-campus food bank, Oh SNAP! Your willingness to share with others in this season will be greatly appreciated.

Look forward to seeing you there!


With best wishes,

Lisa A. Rossbacher, Ph.D.
President
