Campus Community,

I am pleased to announce that Dr. Peg Blake has joined Humboldt State University as our new Vice President for Enrollment Management and Student Affairs. I believe she will be a wonderful addition to our campus.

Peg comes to us from Southern Oregon University, where she was Dean of Enrollment Services and Registrar. Prior to her work at Southern Oregon, she served as Vice President for Student Affairs at Boise State University. She also has served as President of the National Association of Student Personnel Administrators.

Peg earned her Ph.D. in Post-Secondary Education Administration from University of Nebraska-Lincoln. She also earned an MBA with a focus on marketing and a bachelor's degree in Anthropology from the same institution.

The search committee and many of those who met Peg during her campus visit were highly impressed with her clear commitment to students, the breadth and depth of her experience, and her collaborative style. It was clear that Peg knew much about Humboldt State and genuinely wanted to be part of this community.

We finalized Peg's appointment just prior to the winter break, and she has already begun work. In fact, today is her first day.

I would like to thank the members of the search committee for their hard work and diligence throughout this process. I would also like to thank Edward "Buzz" Webb for returning to campus to serve as Interim Vice President. He has been an enormous help in guiding the division through this transition.

I invite you to join me for a campus reception to welcome Peg, and to thank Buzz, at 10 a.m., Friday, Feb. 4 in the College Creek Great Hall.

Sincerely,


Rollin C. Richmond
President
