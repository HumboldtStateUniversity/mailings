Dear Colleagues and Students:

Yesterday, ten Humboldt students were displaced from their apartments in downtown Arcata after a fire broke out about 2:25 a.m. Many across campus have been working with these students to help them figure out how to move ahead.

The students have been offered the opportunity to stay in University Housing for a week without charge and are receiving meal points for this time period. Textbooks and school supplies are being replaced, instructors have been notified, and many other personal needs are being attended to.

Many have asked how they can help. An account is set up in the Sponsored Programs Foundation to help these students get back on their feet. If you would like to donate to this fund, please send your money to the HSU Gift Processing Center with a notation that it is for the "Student Life Trust" fund.

Thank you for responding to these students' needs.

Sincerely,


Rollin C. Richmond
President
