Staff and Faculty,

Please plan to join us on Thursday, Sept. 8 for a special Staff Appreciation Barbecue before Humboldt State's nationally televised football game. The event will be hosted by Intercollegiate Athletics and the Office of the President.

All staff and faculty are invited. The barbecue will begin at 3:30 p.m. at Redwood Bowl Plaza and continue until the 5 p.m. kickoff. A ticket to the barbecue and the game is being sent to you by campus mail.

The game, versus Central Washington, will be aired live nationwide by CBS Sports Network. It will be a great opportunity to promote our university to a broad audience. In addition to the game itself, CBS will air footage of campus, a new HSU commercial highlighting academics, and an interview with longtime faculty member and NCAA Division II Women's Rowing Coach of the Year Robin Meiggs.

The campus as a whole will remain open and classes will not be cancelled. Campus managers will evaluate whether their offices can be closed to allow staff to attend the barbecue. If not, they have been asked to be flexible to allow as many staff as possible to participate.

Please be prepared for parking challenges and traffic congestion on the day of the game. Because it will be televised, the game is likely to draw a larger-than-usual crowd, and with a Thursday game there will be many more people on campus all at one time. In addition, the parking lot behind Founders Hall and Van Matre will be closed for use by CBS crews, except for disabled and service vehicle parking.

We are arranging for parking and shuttle service from the Arcata Community Center and Arcata High School for community members attending the game. If you can avoid driving a personal vehicle to work that day, please do. To help, the packet being mailed to you by Athletics will include a one-day JackPass, which will allow you to use regional transit for free on the day of the game.

If you have not received a packet with a ticket and JackPass by early next week, please email Stephanie Lane in Intercollegiate Athletics at Stephanie.Lane@humboldt.edu.

I hope to see you at the barbecue and the game.

Sincerely,


Rollin C. Richmond
President
