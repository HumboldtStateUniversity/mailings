Dear Colleagues:

Dr. Robert Gunsalus, HSU's Vice President for University Advancement, has accepted the position as Vice President for University Relations at Santa Clara University. His final day at HSU will be August 27.

Rob has been a valuable leader at HSU, and I am sorry to see him leave. I wish him the very best in his new role.

Under Rob's leadership, our University Advancement division has been significantly reorganized, and efforts to bring more resources to campus and better market HSU are showing strong results. Alumni, families and friends are supporting the University in record numbers. In fact, even in a difficult economy when philanthropy has declined nationwide, our end-of-year reports will show a dramatic increase in private giving to HSU, with total giving expected to top $5 million. That includes more than $2 million raised for our endowment.

Rob has also brought a number of good people to the division, and I expect they will continue the momentum. HSU will be increasingly dependent on non-state funds in the years to come, and increased private giving will be needed to support our academic mission well into the future.

I am working to form a search committee to identify the new Vice President for Advancement. The search will begin in the fall and will be led by Burt Nordstrom, Vice President for Administrative Affairs.

In the meantime, Frank Whitlatch, Associate Vice President for Marketing & Communications, will serve as the Interim Vice President.

Sincerely,

Rollin C. Richmond
President
