Dear Colleagues,

It is my pleasure to announce the appointment of Traci Ferdolage as the new Associate Vice President for Facilities Management.

Traci has been the Senior Director of Facilities Planning & Design since 2011. Prior to that, she worked as an Assistant Program Manager, Project Manager and Design Manager within the Facilities and Construction Management departments. Traci was involved in the construction of the Behavioral and Social Sciences Building, Physical Education Project, Schatz Energy Research Center, and College Creek Marketplace.

Tim Moxon has diligently served as the Senior Director of Facilities Management, as well as other senior positions within Plant Operations, for over 30 years. Tim recently announced his retirement. I would like to personally extend my thanks to Tim for his outstanding service to the University Community and wish him the very best in his future endeavors. A retirement reception will be held for Tim after the holiday break.

Please join me in welcoming Traci Ferdolage as the new Associate Vice President for Facilities Management and in thanking Tim Moxon for his exceptional service to the University.

Sincerely,

Joyce Lopes
Vice President of Administrative Affairs
