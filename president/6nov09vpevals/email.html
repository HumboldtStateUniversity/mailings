Dear Colleagues and Students:

We are initiating a pilot project for the administrative review of vice presidents. In addition to the annual evaluation process, each vice president will participate in an administrative review process every three years. Vice Presidents Rob Gunsalus and Steve Butler are being reviewed this year. Included in this process is the opportunity for the campus community to provide input. If you would like to do so, please e-mail me (rollinr@humboldt.edu) or submit written material to me no later than January 8, 2010. The details for this new process may be viewed at http://www.humboldt.edu/~hsuhr/employee/evaluation/.
