Faculty & Staff Holiday Party - Reminder

December 5, 2011
 

Friends and Colleagues,

The holiday season is just about here, and this year we are planning to recognize your good work with a campus-wide party for all faculty and staff.

Please join the fun any time between 4 and 6 p.m. on Wednesday, December 7 in the Kate Buchanan Room. Unfortunately, I will miss the event because I must attend a meeting at the Chancellor's Office.

The event will feature music, appetizers, desserts and beverages.

I hope you are able to attend.

Sincerely,
