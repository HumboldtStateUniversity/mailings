Dear Students and Colleagues:

I am pleased to inform you about a number of campus projects that will be funded this year through the Humboldt Loyalty Fund and the Parents & Family Fund.

These funds are made up of gifts from thousands of alumni and family members, and we are very grateful for their decision to support Humboldt State University. Nearly 4,000 alumni and more than 700 family members made a gift to HSU last year, and their gifts are making a real difference in the educational experience of students here.

This year, the Humboldt Loyalty Fund and the Parents & Family Fund are providing:

  • Tanks and equipment at our Marine Lab in Trinidad, in the amount of $10,000
  • A pilot project with the Journalism Department for students and faculty to help provide local news coverage on KHSU, in the amount of $15,000
  • A variety of scholarships, in the amount of $27,000
  • Support of the Humboldt Alumni Career Network, which gives alumni access to premier online career services (students may also access the services, through a partnership with the Career Center), in the amount of $7,500
  • Assistance in bringing academic speakers to campus, in the amount of $10,000
  • Assistance in sending students and faculty to make presentations at academic conferences, in the amount of $100,000
  • Dean's Project Funds for special initiatives, equipment and other needs in the Colleges and Library, in the amount of $75,000

These two funds are now well-established, and going forward, I have decided to create a more formal process for the campus community to submit proposals. Details and criteria are being developed, and information will be distributed on campus before the end of the fall semester. We plan to accept proposals next spring for the 2011-12 academic year.

These gifts are an important demonstration of support for what we do at Humboldt State. It is clear that our alumni had a wonderful experience when they were students here, and that current family members value the education and personal attention their students are receiving. We should all be proud of that.

If you know an alumnus or a family member who has made a gift to HSU, please take the time to thank them. Tell them what a difference their support makes.

And if you are also an HSU donor - Thank you very much.

Sincerely,

Rollin C. Richmond
President
