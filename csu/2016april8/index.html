All Classes to Continue as Normal

April 8, 2016
Sent on Behalf of the CSU Chancellors Office

Dear Students,

I am pleased to announce that the California State University and California Faculty Association have reached a tentative agreement. With this announcement, all courses scheduled for the remainder of the term will continue as normal.

I know and regret that the threat of a strike – called by the CFA – has been disconcerting for students. Indeed, it has been so for faculty, staff and administrators as well. I am very pleased to have this behind us now.

With this announcement, we can increase investment in our faculty’s compensation. We reached earlier agreements with our staff. All of these investments in faculty and staff are investments in our students and their learning environment – our highest priority.

Today’s announcement is a good moment for our university community, but the work continues. We need your voices – as students – to join with ours in asking our champions across the state and in the legislature to Stand with CSU. Together, we will create lasting success for your future.

I wish you well for the remainder of this academic year and beyond.

Sincerely,

Tim White
Chancellor
