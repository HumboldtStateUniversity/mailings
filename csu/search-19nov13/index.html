Open letter to the Humboldt State community:

As Chair of the Humboldt State presidential search committee, I have the privilege of working with the Humboldt State campus community, the California State University (CSU) Board of Trustees, and Chancellor White as we seek the next President to lead Humboldt State University (HSU) into its second century. After twelve years of outstanding and visionary leadership, President Rollin Richmond will be retiring in June of 2014.

This search process included an open campus forum on October 28, 2013 that was intended to provide feedback to the Search Committee, the Board of Trustees and the Chancellor about the leadership attributes of the next President and the current state of affairs at Humboldt.

To encourage the public dialogue and input from the stakeholders in Humboldt State we have created an online form that allows the discussion of qualities on the next President to continue. I hope to have your voice be part of the search process and have your voice heard at http://humboldt.edu/presidentsearch/search-forum-comments .

Thank you in advance for sharing your input on the next President of Humboldt State.

Sincerely,

Larry Norton
California State University Trustee
Chair, Humboldt State University Presidential Search Committee
