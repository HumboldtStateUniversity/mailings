Dear Student,

Envisioning where HSU needs to be in 5 years is an exciting challenge, and it can’t be done without the opinions and ideas of students.

That’s why you may be receiving an invitation to participate in a campus survey. It’s relatively short and includes questions focused on the university’s key priorities:

  • Making sure students' academic experience is successful
  • Supporting a diverse and inclusive campus community
  • Budgeting financial resources to fulfill the university’s mission

The survey also includes open-ended questions, providing another opportunity to share thoughts about what the next few years will look like. These same topics will be the focus of upcoming brainstorming sessions, which students are encouraged to attend.

Although the survey is voluntary, responses will ultimately help develop a clearer sense of students’ priorities and overall vision for the university.

Please weigh in and help plan HSU’s future.

For questions about the strategic planning process, feel free to contact me at noah.zerbe@humboldt.edu. If you have any questions about the survey, please contact Lisa Castellino at lisa.castellino@humboldt.edu.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,

Noah Zerbe
Chair, University Senate
Co-Chair, Strategic Planning Steering Committee
