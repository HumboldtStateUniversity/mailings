A Message from Humboldt State University

September 27, 2018

$1.2 Million of Graduation Initiative 2025 Funding to Support Twenty Faculty Searches This Year

Dear HSU Students, Faculty, and Staff,

On behalf of the Student Success Alliance committee, I am pleased to announce that Academic Affairs will launch twenty new faculty searches during the 2018-2019 Academic Year. Below you will find a list of the planned searches that span CAHSS, CNRS, CPS, and the Library. These searches will support HSU’s commitment to diverse faculty hiring, continued development of first-year learning communities, and environmental responsibility and social justice.

The twenty faculty searches were made possible by funding from:

  1. $1.2 million from 2018-2019 Graduation Initiative 2025 monies
    • $825,000 minimum allocation for faculty hiring per California State Bill 840 that established this year’s GI2025 funding
    • $375,000 increase in the faculty hiring allocation recommended by the HSU Student Success Alliance (visit https://grad2025.humboldt.edu/ for more details)
  2. $350,000 additional investment from the Academic Affairs base budget approved by me

It is anticipated that twenty new faculty members will join their respective departments for the start of classes in Fall 2019. Here are the planned faculty searches:

Environmental Studies – Environmental Justice
Native American Studies – Generalist/Support for place-based learning communities
English – Writing Practices, Digital Rhetoric with emphasis on Latinx
Politics – Global Politics
Theatre, Film, & Dance – Scenography
Anthropology – Applied Culture
Journalism and Mass Communication – Public Relations
Environmental Science & Management – Environmental Law, Policy and Conflict Resolution
Mathematics – Interdisciplinary Applied Math with emphasis on Student Success
Forestry – Forest Operations with expertise in Tribal Issues and CA forest practice
Chemistry – Physical or Inorganic Chemist
Biology – Genetics/Developmental Biology
LSEE/Education – Elementary Education
Kinesiology & Recreation Administration – Physical Education
Kinesiology & Recreation Administration – Exercise Science
Business – Finance
Child Development – Child Development
Library – First Year Experience & Student Success
Library – College of Arts, Humanities & Social Sciences Librarian
Library – College of Professional Studies Librarian
