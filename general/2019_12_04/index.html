A Message from Humboldt State University

December 4, 2019

Loss of HSU Student Kenneth Xiong

Dear University Community,

We are sad to share news of the loss of Humboldt State University student Kenneth Xiong. We extend our condolences to all who have been affected by his loss, particularly his family, roommates, and friends.

Kenneth, who was a second year student pursuing his degree in Fisheries Biology, died on November 30, 2019. He was 19 years old. Kenneth was previously in the Klamath Connection learning community, which is a place-based learning community in the College of Natural Resources & Sciences.
