A Message from Humboldt State University

February 24, 2017

Homeward Bound Bus for Spring Break

Dear HSU students:

Are you still trying to figure out how to get home and back to campus for Spring Break? If so, Parking & Commuter Services may be able to help.

The Homeward Bound Bus Charter Program is a new service that provides students with discounted round-trip transportation from Arcata directly to San Francisco or Los Angeles.

Bus service will be offered on Saturday, March 11 to San Francisco and Los Angeles, with a return trip to Arcata on Saturday, March 18. Round trip fare per student is $90 to Los Angeles and $70 to San Francisco. Offering lower rates than Greyhound, this new Parking & Commuter Services program is a great deal. Free WiFi is included, too!

To purchase your ticket go to the Student Center at the myHumboldt portal. Select “Make a Payment” under the Finances area, and then select “Make a Payment” again. Choose the Homeward Bound Bus Charter Program from the menu and then continue through the reservation and purchasing process.

We encourage you to reserve your space as soon as possible as seats
are limited.

For more information about the program, contact Krista Paddock in Parking & Commuter Services at (707) 826-4111 or visit humboldt.edu/parking/content/homeward-bound-program.
