A Message from Cal Poly Humboldt

November 18, 2024

Informal Time Off for Fall Break

Dear Colleagues,
It is with great appreciation that I write today to thank you for all you've done this semester to support and advance Cal Poly Humboldt's mission. I have been so impressed with your dedication to this wonderful university, and I am thankful for your commitment to providing a positive and transformative educational experience for our students.
With gratitude, I am pleased to provide staff the approval to end their work three hours before the end of their workday on Wednesday, November 27, 2024. To ensure that necessary work is completed, your appropriate administrator must approve this paid Informal Time Off (ITO).
Please use this link for information and pay instructions on using ITO, and be sure to refer to this myHumboldt message if you have any questions about the holiday-related campus closure on Friday, November 29.
Thank you once again for all your contributions to our campus and the students and community we serve.
I wish you a safe and restful Fall Break!
Sincerely,
Michael E. Spagna, Ph.D.
Interim President
