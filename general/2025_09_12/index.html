A Message from Cal Poly Humboldt

September 12, 2025

Kick Off the Fun with College Colors Day and the Kickball Tournament!

Get ready, Lumberjacks—next week is all about kickball, campus pride, and community fun! Eight teams of students, alumni, faculty, and staff will face off in the ultimate showdown for the kickball trophy.
The action kicks off at Redwood Bowl at Noon Monday through Thursday:
  • Monday, Sept. 15
    OH HEY HEY! vs. Kicking it Old School
    Academic Affairs vs. Academic Affairs
  • Tuesday, Sept. 16
    Lumberjack Legends vs. Jack Attack
    Alumni vs. Students
  • Wednesday, Sept. 17
    2 Legit 2 Kick vs. Pitch, Please!
    Athletics/Advancement vs. Academic Affairs
  • Thursday, Sept. 18
    A Case of the Runs vs. Kickin’ Assets
    Enrollment Management & Student Affairs vs. Administrative Affairs
Then, on Friday, Sept. 19, it all comes together on the Upper Playing Field (near Redwood Bowl) from Noon until 6 for College Colors Day! The playoffs and the championship game will decide who takes home the glory.
But that’s not all—we’ll have free food, music, face painting, and games for the whole family. Wear your green and gold and help us turn the field into a sea of Lumberjack pride.
And if you can’t catch every game in person, follow President Spagna on Instagram for daily Instagram Stories with tournament results and winners.
The stage is set. The trash talk is heating up. The trophy is waiting. Will a new champion rise—or will the two-time reigning champions, 2 Legit 2 Kick, prove unstoppable yet again?
Don’t miss it—this year’s College Colors Day is going to be a kick!
