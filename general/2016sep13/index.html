A Message from Humboldt State University

September 13, 2016

Reminder: HSU Athletics Assessment is Today at 11 a.m.

Dear HSU Students,

Don't forget that today at 11:00 a.m. in Goodwin Forum, NHE 102, is your opportunity to offer input on HSU Athletics.

Strategic Edge consultants are on campus today and tomorrow and they are preparing an assessment of Athletics. They will offer suggestions and recommendations based on observations, data, and bests practices from across the country.

If you cannot attend the event today, you can share your comments using the online form at http://www.humboldt.edu/president/node/169
