A Message from Cal Poly Humboldt

July 3, 2023

New Cal Poly Humboldt Website Launches July 5

Cal Poly Humboldt’s newly redesigned humboldt.edu website will launch on Wednesday, July 5.
The comprehensive update to the design and structure of the University's primary web presence is part of the Brand Initiative work with partner agency SimpsonScarborough.
Built with a primary focus on enhancing the digital experience for prospective students, the website makes the content they’re looking for easily accessible. It showcases our academic programs, research initiatives, campus life, and other resources. With streamlined menus, improved functionality, and a responsive layout that works well on all devices, the updated design creates a visually engaging experience that aligns with our new brand standards.
After the redesigned humboldt.edu launches, all other University sites will be updated with the new Cal Poly Humboldt logo, colors, and fonts that are part of the new brand. Then as part of Phase 2 of the website refresh, other sites will begin the transition to the updated content management system.
Thank you to all who provided feedback during the most recent review period in May. Many updates and changes were made reflecting that input.
This new website is the culmination of many months of work and opens exciting possibilities to share our story and showcase how our institution shapes futures. The site is meant to be iterative, and we invite you to explore the platform and continue to provide feedback.
There is much more to come. Your ideas and participation are appreciated as we further develop the new Cal Poly Humboldt brand.
