A Message from Humboldt State University

March 22, 2018

Time Change: WSCUC Review Team Exit Meeting

Dear University Community:

Thank you for all of the input you have given to the WSCUC Review Team on Wednesday and Thursday.

The visiting team apologizes for having to move the Friday, March 23 Exit Meeting (at which they will provide a summary of their findings and recommendations to the University community) in the Native American Forum from the original starting time of 8:00 a.m. to 7:30 a.m. Unfortunately, it is against WSCUC policy to videotape the meeting.

Some of the review team members need to get back home due to other commitments (including a WSCUC review on their own campus), and they are concerned about being able to make their connecting flights (two members had their San Francisco to Arcata flights cancelled on Tuesday and the weather is getting worse).

Sincerely,

Rock Braithwaite, Ed.D.
