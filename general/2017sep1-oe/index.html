A Message from Humboldt State University

September 1, 2017

Benefits Open Enrollment Period: September 11 – October 6

To: Benefit Eligible Employees

The CSU annual Open Enrollment period will occur September 11, 2017 through October 6, 2017. Open Enrollment is your annual opportunity to review and update your current benefits for the upcoming year. You can enroll, change, or cancel CalPERS health, dental, vision, Flexcash, Dependent Care Reimbursement (DCRA), Health Care Reimbursement (HCRA) or voluntary benefits plans. The effective date for all changes/enrollments made during the Open Enrollment period will be January 1, 2018.

For 2018, we are pleased to announce that the premiums for most medical plans will be decreasing. Please carefully review the 2018 Health Premium Rate Sheet. The level of coverage for both Health Maintenance Organization (HMO) plans, benefits and network providers in Humboldt County are the same, and are facilitated through the Independent Practice Association (IPA) in Eureka; the Preferred Provider Organization (PPO) plans are administered directly by Anthem Blue Cross.

CSU benefit eligible employees now have the option to enroll into the VSP Premier Plan, which offers a higher allowance for frames and contacts, new glasses or contacts every calendar year, and more! Please see csuactives.vspforme.com for more information. Click “View Your Plan” for a comparison of the standard VSP plan with VSP Premier.

We are also happy to announce that you now have online access to your benefit enrollment information through your MyHumboldt Portal! You can now review your health enrollments from anywhere, 24/7. Learn more about how to find your Benefits Summary here.

  • The Open Enrollment Benefits Communication–for the 2018 plan year.
  • The 2018 Health Premium Rate Sheet.
  • To make a health plan change ONLY, complete the highlighted section of the 2018 Plan Change Only Form. To add or delete dependents, complete the Benefits Enrollment Worksheet. Please note that if you are adding/deleting dependents and changing health plans, you must complete BOTH forms.
  • Health Care Reimbursement (HCRA) and Dependent Care Reimbursement Account (DCRA) enrollment form. Please note that you must re-enroll each year in order to maintain this benefit.
  • All information regarding Open Enrollment will be available on the Benefits Website.

All open enrollment forms with original signatures must be hand-delivered to the Human Resources office, Siemens Hall, Room 212, by 5:00 p.m., Friday,
October 6, 2017.

Please plan to attend the annual Benefits Fair scheduled for Monday, September 25, 9:00 a.m. – 11:00 a.m., in Goodwin Forum. HSU Benefits staff will be available during the Benefits Fair to answer questions.
