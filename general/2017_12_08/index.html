Today we are heartbroken to share news of the loss of Humboldt State University student Erin Henry. We want to express our deepest sympathies to her friends, family, and the many faculty and staff who knew her.

A Theatre Arts major, Erin, 22, was active as a performer, writer, and organizer for many productions in the Theatre, Film & Dance Department. She was also a social justice advocate and involved in Dell Arteâ€™s project "Jane Doe in Wonderland."

She leaves behind friends from campus and the community whose lives she has touched. We ask you to keep her loved ones in your thoughts and comfort each other during this difficult time.

Losing a member of the Humboldt State community can affect each of us differently. If you feel the need for support, please know that help is available.

Students are encouraged to contact Counseling & Psychological Services at 707.826.3236. The Dean of Students Office can also be reached at 707.826.3504. Staff or faculty seeking additional support may utilize the Employee Assistance Program at 707.443.7358.
