A Message from Cal Poly Humboldt

May 6, 2026

Staff Support Needed at Spring 2026 Commencement

Spring 2026 Commencement
Saturday, May 16 | Redwood Bowl
Spring Commencement is one of the most meaningful milestones for our students, and the Spring 2026 ceremonies are fast approaching on Saturday, May 16, at the Redwood Bowl.
To ensure a safe, smooth, and celebratory experience for our graduates and their guests, we urgently need at least 30 more volunteers. Simply put—Commencement cannot happen without your support.
Commencement Support
We are asking employees across campus—including administrators, faculty (including coaches), staff, student assistants, and volunteers—to assist with behind-the-scenes roles such as:
  • Greeting guests
  • Ushering
  • Shuttle and wheelchair escort support
  • Parking and traffic assistance
  • Supporting graduate and faculty/staff lineup areas
Full-day and half-day shifts are available. If you can help, please coordinate with your supervisor and submit the Commencement work interest and availability form by Friday, May 8. Supervisors and/or managers with any questions on how to compensate staff who choose to work Commencement should contact the Human Resources Department.
All staff supporting Commencement will be given a name badge and asked to check in at the designated Staff & Vendor Check-In area on the day of the event before their shift. Shift details and assignments will be shared closer to the event.
Breakfast and lunch will be available to all employees working the event, and a designated hospitality room will be available for staff to take breaks comfortably.

We recognize this is a busy time of year, but your participation plays a vital role in honoring our students’ achievements. Please consider signing up and encouraging your colleagues to do the same.
Thank you in advance for your support in making Commencement a memorable and successful event for our graduates.
