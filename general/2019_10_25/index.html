A Message from Humboldt State University

October 25, 2019

Campus Safety Bulletin: Hate Speech Flyer

Dear HSU Community,

On Thursday evening, UPD received notice of a religiously intolerant flyer that contained hate speech.

The campus was immediately checked for additional flyers. Last night and this morning, the few remaining flyers were taken down.

While there are no suspects at this time, UPD is investigating the matter. The original flyer and details about the incident have been shared with our federal partners in order to assist with indicating if this is a broader or more widespread issue or to identify the perpetrator.

We have notified our counseling center, care teams, cultural centers, and our behavioral intervention team to be on alert to support our students.

If you have information about this incident or find more flyers, please contact University Police at 707-826-5555.
