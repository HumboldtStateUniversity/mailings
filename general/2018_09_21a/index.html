A Message from Humboldt State University

September 21, 2018

Report from Student Fee Committee

The Task Force on Student Fees completed its work in May, and its report is now final and available for download.

Read the report (pdf)

The Task Force on Student Fees was created by the Associated Students and President Rossbacher in Spring 2018. Its charge was to “Examine mandatory student fees at Humboldt State University, determine whether they are serving the current needs of students, and make recommendations for any future changes.” The Task Force agreed that its goals were to 1) ensure the student fees are meeting student needs and 2) keep mandatory fees as low as possible to ensure affordability.

The report includes a series of recommendations by the Task Force, as well as an explanation of the various types of student fees at HSU.
