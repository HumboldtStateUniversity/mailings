WSCUC Student Open Forum today 5:10 p.m.

Hello Student Community,

As a reminder, the WSCUC STUDENT OPEN FORUM IS TONIGHT at 5:10 p.m. in the Native American Forum/BSS 122. Join us if you are interested and available!

As you may know, HSU is accredited by “WSCUC,” the WASC Senior College & University Commission (formerly known as simply WASC; or the Western Association of Schools and Colleges). HSU accreditation status is vitally important – through the process of self-study and feedback, HSU and the visiting team will ensure that institutional objectives are clearly defined, measured, assessed and achieved.

Learn more about the accreditation process and reports here.

Feedback can be provided in person at open forums, at Associated Student meetings, as well as online.

When: Wednesday, March 21, 2018 5:10pm – 5:35pm Pacific Time
Where: Native American Forum / BSS 122
