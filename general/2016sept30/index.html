A Message from Humboldt State University

September 30, 2016

Annual Campus Security Report is Available

Dear University Community,

I am pleased to announce that Humboldt State University has completed its 2016 Annual Security Report. Submitted in compliance with the federal Jeanne Clery Act, the Annual Security Report is a comprehensive assessment of crime on our campus as well as outlying properties used by Humboldt State University. The report additionally provides for historical comparisons with previous years.

We take the safety of our students, faculty, staff, and visitors very seriously. This report is an important component of those efforts and illustrates our attention to detail and transparency when it comes to reporting crime on our campus.

You may find our Annual Security Report at humboldt.edu/police/clery-act-campus-safety-reports, or you may obtain a printed copy at the University Police Department.

Sincerely,

Donn Peterson
University Police Chief
