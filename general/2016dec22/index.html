A Message from Humboldt State University

December 22, 2016

Homeward Bound Bus Charter Program

Dear HSU student:

Still trying to figure out how you will make your way back to Humboldt State University for the start of the spring semester? If so, Parking & Commuter Services may be able to help.

Parking & Commuter Services is kicking off the Homeward Bound Bus Charter Program. This new transportation service will provide students with a discounted one-way trip from Los Angeles or San Francisco directly to the campus. Service will be offered on January 13, 2017 only. Trips cost $45 per student from Los Angeles and $35 per student from San Francisco. This is a great deal for students and costs less than published fares with Greyhound.

To make your reservation today, simply click on this link: https://commerce.cashnet.com/cashnetg/selfserve/BrowseCatalog.aspx?CNAME=HOMEWARD+PRG and continue through the reservation and purchasing process. Once your reservation is complete, you will receive a confirmation letter from Parking & Commuter Services which provides more detail regarding the pickup locations and times, baggage restrictions, and general trip information concerning how to locate the charter bus, the intended route and expected stops for rest, stretching and food as well as arrival in Arcata.

We encourage you to reserve your space as soon as possible as seats are limited.

For more information regarding the program, contact Krista Paddock in Parking & Commuter Services at (707) 826-4111 or visit our website at humboldt.edu/parking/content/homeward-bound-program.
