A Message from Humboldt State University

October 30, 2020

Loss of HSU Student Matthew Martinez

Dear University Community,

We are sad to share news of the loss of Humboldt State University student Matthew Martinez. We extend our condolences to all who have been affected by his loss, particularly his family members and friends.

Matthew was a Biology major and was a freshman. He died on October 21 and was 19 years old. The University has been in close contact with Matthew's family and will continue to assist them during this tragic time.

Matthew's death is a difficult loss for our community, especially now, when we are forced to remain socially distant from one another. Despite these circumstances, HSU is committed to supporting the needs of our university community. For those who are struggling with grief or otherwise, Counseling & Psychological Services (CAPS) is available virtually 24/7 for support. If you are interested in speaking with a counselor please contact CAPS at (707) 826-3236.

If you need help facilitating a conversation with your faculty or others on campus, the Dean of Students Office may be reached at (707) 826-3504 or dos@humboldt.edu. Staff or faculty seeking additional support may utilize the Employee Assistance Program at (707) 443-7358.
