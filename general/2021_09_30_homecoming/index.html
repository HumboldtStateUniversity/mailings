The Humboldt State community is invited to HSU’s Homecoming Week, October 4 – 9! This is an opportunity for faculty, staff, and students to come together and celebrate all that it means to be a Lumberjack! There will be free virtual and in-person events, with COVID-19 precautions in place to ensure the health and wellbeing of our campus community.
To kick off the week, stop by the Student Activities Center (SAC) Quad for the Homecoming Pep Rally on Monday, October 4 from 11 a.m. – 2 p.m. Show your HSU spirit and enjoy the yard games, giveaways, free food, and a DJ!
For a full week of amazing activities, go to homecoming.humboldt.edu or follow @hsu_studentlife on instagram. Email osl@humboldt.edu with questions.
