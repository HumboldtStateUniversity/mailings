A Message from Humboldt State University

October 30, 2015

Provost Candidate - Open Forum for Students

Dr. Alexander Enyedi, candidate for the Provost and Vice President of Academic Affairs position, will visit campus on November 18 and 19. Dr. Enyedi was unavailable earlier due to an extended commitment overseas.

Students are invited to attend the open forum for Dr. Enyedi. An abbreviated biography for Dr. Enyedi is linked below.

Dr. Enyedi will discuss “What does a Provost do and how does that relate to student success at HSU?” followed by a time for questions. Please bring questions written on a 3x5 card or plan to write a question on cards that will be distributed at the forum.

As with the other candidates, we ask for your support in welcoming Dr. Enyedi to Humboldt State University. While he is being interviewed for the position, please keep in mind that he is also interviewing us, and our goal is for him to leave campus saying “I really want that job.”

The search committee members value your input on this important decision. If you attend the open forum, please complete the short survey in the link below following the forum. All input received by noon on Friday, November 20, will be considered.

Dr. Alexander Enyedi – Thursday, November 19, 11:00-11:50 a.m., University Center Banquet Room (next to the Depot).

Survey Link: https://www.surveymonkey.com/r/AlexanderEnyedi
