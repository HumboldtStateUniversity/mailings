A Message from Humboldt State University

October 9, 2017

Support for Those Affected by Fires

Campus Community,

Many in our Humboldt State University community have family and friends who have been impacted by the fires in Sonoma and Napa counties, as well as in other California regions.

Our thoughts are with you and we hope your loved ones are safe.

If you are currently traveling in an affected area, please be cautious and stay updated on current conditions. In particular, be aware of official announcements such as road closures and changing traffic conditions.

Students who need support during this time are urged to consider contacting the Dean of Students office in Siemens Hall 211 or at 707.826.3504, or Counseling & Psychological Services at 707.826.3236. Staff or faculty seeking support may utilize the Employee Assistance Program at 707.443.7358.
