A Message from Humboldt State University

September 15, 2015

Dear Students,

California State University Chancellor Timothy White will visit HSU on Tuesday, September 22.

During his visit, he will hold an open forum in the Kate Buchanan Room from 2:45-4:15 p.m. where he will outline the CSU vision of strategic intent and will take questions. The forum is open to all students, employees, and community members.

Chancellor White will also visit various parts of campus and take part in activities throughout the day, with a focus on academics and student engagement.
