Dear Students,

You are invited to a special forum with Humboldt State University President Lisa A. Rossbacher, hosted by the Lumberjack student newspaper. The event will be at 3:30 p.m., Tuesday, Oct. 7 in the University Center’s Kate Buchanan Room.

Questions for President Rossbacher may be submitted in advance to the Lumberjack at thejack@humboldt.edu or by visiting the Lumberjack’s table during Saturday’s football game. There will also be a chance for attendees to ask questions during the forum.

This is President Rossbacher’s first year at Humboldt State. She started in July, and has been focused on learning as much as possible about the people and programs on campus.

You can learn more about President Rossbacher at the President’s Office website or by reading the recent Humboldt magazine article about her. You can also follow her on Instagram and Twitter at “HSUpres.”
