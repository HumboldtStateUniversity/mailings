A Message from the Provost Search Committee

September 29, 2015

Provost Candidates - Open Forum for HSU Staff and Faculty

Dear Colleagues:

Four candidates for the Provost and Vice President of Academic Affairs position will visit campus over the next couple of weeks. All faculty and staff, as well as interested community members, are invited to attend the Open Forums for each of the candidates. Abbreviated biographies for each candidate are linked to their names, below.

Candidates will give a 15-minute oral presentation, and will then respond to pre-submitted questions. The purpose of including pre-submitted questions is to ensure that a broad range of campus topics and interests are fairly represented at all the forums. If you would like to submit a question for this part of the forum, please submit them via the Google form. After pre-submitted questions, there will be a period for open questions from the audience.

All of us on the search committee and President Rossbacher ask for your support in welcoming each of these candidates to Humboldt State University. While they are being interviewed for the position, please keep in mind that they are also interviewing us, and our goal is for every one of them to leave campus saying “I really want this job and to be part of this community!”

The search committee members value your input on this important decision. If you attend the open forum, please complete the short survey for each candidate using the links listed below.
