A Message from Humboldt State University

January 13, 2021

Reminder: 2021 Spring Welcome From President Jackson

Don’t forget the Virtual 2021 Spring Welcome is tomorrow, January 14 from 8:30-10 a.m.

This will be an opportunity to learn about important campus updates and exciting initiatives that are taking place at HSU!

President Jackson will start his Spring Welcome Address at 8:30 a.m., and immediately following at 9:15 a.m. there will be concurrent breakout sessions with the VPs from each division giving updates about the topics below. There will also be a newly added session in the afternoon about the enhancement of student services and the transition of services previously overseen by University Center. You will need to register in advance to attend a session, and each one will be recorded.

The links to Spring Welcome activities will be accessible on the President’s Office website the morning of the event. To register for a breakout session, click on the Zoom link associated with it below.

Time permitting, presenters will be able to address questions about the below topics and others. Submit your questions in advance using this Google form.
