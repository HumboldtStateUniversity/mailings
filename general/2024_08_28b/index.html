A Message from Cal Poly Humboldt

August 28, 2024

You’re Invited to Lumberjack Weekend!

Get ready to show your Green and Gold spirit for Lumberjack Weekend!
October 17–20
Lumberjack Weekend is all about building pride and celebrating our Lumberjack family of students, staff, faculty, alumni, families, and friends.
New this year, all participants must register. Students, staff and faculty have the option to register for free or for a fee with a t-shirt. Space is limited so be sure to register ASAP!
Weekend Highlights
Lumberjack Weekend activities begin Thursday evening with the Kickoff Party at 6 p.m. at the University Quad. For an additional cost, head over to the E-40 concert in Van Duzer Theatre at 9 p.m.
Other highlights include the Fall Maker’s Market featuring student and staff craft vendors on Friday from 11 a.m. - 2 p.m. at the University Quad, and a Forever Humboldt Arcata Marsh Walk on Sunday morning at 10 a.m.
Lumberjack Weekend Block Party
Saturday from 4:00 p.m. - 8 p.m. in the G11 and FS9 parking lots.
This year’s block party will feature The Beach Boys as our headliner, followed by a dazzling drone show by Skyworx! Enjoy a variety of food trucks and beverages, with beer and wine available for those with valid ID.
