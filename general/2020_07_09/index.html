A Message from Humboldt State University

July 9, 2020

Loss of HSU Student Eunice Estrada

Dear University Community,

We are sad to share news of the loss of Humboldt State University student Eunice Estrada. We extend our condolences to all who have been affected by her loss, particularly her family members and friends.

Eunice was a Kinesiology major and had graduated in May. She died on June 28 and was 23 years old. As a member of the Swing Dance Club, Eunice was actively involved during her time at HSU. She had also planned to work as a physical therapist. We have reached out to the family and will continue to provide support.
