A Message from Humboldt State University

April 19, 2019

Associated Students Elections Suspended

Dear HSU Students,

The Associated Students Elections Commission suspended voting for Associated Students elections after technical problems were discovered in the voting system. Issues included links to online polls being emailed to former students as well as some current students not receiving the email.

As a result, voting ended Thursday, April 17 at 11 a.m. and all votes have been voided.

The AS Elections Commission met and voted to recommend that the AS Board of Directors approve a new voting period of Tuesday, April 23 to Thursday, April 25. These new election dates will be officially announced and posted on the AS website at associatedstudents.humboldt.edu after the AS Board of Directors meeting on Monday evening, April 22.

AS is dedicated to uplifting and protecting actions of equity, inclusivity, and understanding. We believe all students should have an equal opportunity to vote.

We apologize for the inconvenience and we appreciate your understanding in this matter.

Sincerely,

Associated Students
