A Message from Humboldt State University

January 19, 2022

Professional Development Day and Polytechnic Student Forum

Dear Campus Community,
On January 13, Humboldt State University staff and faculty gathered virtually and in-person for a successful and collaborative Professional Development Day. Overall, 131 people participated throughout the day of professional development programming, a higher than average turnout. The focus of Professional Development Day included sharing instructional resources, working toward equity and inclusivity, and preparing staff and faculty for the anticipated polytechnic designation.
After a welcome and opening presentation from the Sexual Assault Prevention Committee, staff and faculty were invited to attend 17 unique breakout sessions. Nearly all sessions took place in-person at Founders Hall with the option to attend virtually via Zoom. The presenters included two students, eight faculty, 12 staff, and 13 administrators.
Nine sessions focused on topics of inclusivity and/or equity on campus and seven sessions provided updates from the HSU Polytechnic Implementation Working Group leads. To view the recorded Polytechnic Committee updates, please use the following links:
