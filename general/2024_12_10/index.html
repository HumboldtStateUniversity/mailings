The Search Advisory Committee includes James Woglom, Chair of the University Senate; Daniel Barton and Marissa Ramsier, faculty representatives; Wysdem Singleton and Mara Kravitz, student representatives; Daniel Sealy, Alumni representative; Carmen Bustos-Works, administration representative; Miles Slattery, community advisory representative; Bella Gray, staff representative; and Betty Chinn and Virgil Moorehead, community representatives. Both committees function as one unified group.
The CSU has selected Academic Search as consultants to conduct the presidential search. All members of the campus community are encouraged to send any nominations or expressions of interest to CalPolyHumboldtPresident@academicsearch.org, which will be treated in a confidential manner.
