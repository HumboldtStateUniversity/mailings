A Message from the Provost Search Committee

September 29, 2015

Provost Candidates - Open Forum for Students

Dear Students:

Four candidates for the Provost and Vice President of Academic Affairs position will visit campus during the first two weeks of October. Students are invited to attend open forums for each of the candidates. Each candidate will briefly discuss “What does a Provost do and how does that relate to student success at HSU?” followed by a time for questions. Please bring questions written on a 3x5 card or plan to write a question on cards that will be distributed at the forum. Abbreviated biographies for each candidate are linked to their names, below.

All of us on the search committee and President Rossbacher ask for your support in welcoming each of these candidates to Humboldt State University. While they are being interviewed for the position, please keep in mind that they are also interviewing us, and our goal is for every one of them to leave campus saying “I really want this job and to be part of this community!”

The search committee members value your input on this important decision. If you attend the open forum, please complete the short survey for each candidate using the links listed below.
