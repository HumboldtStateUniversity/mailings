You’re Invited: Accreditation Site Visit Launch Event

Accreditation Site Visit Launch
Wednesday, March 4, 2026
11:30 a.m. – 1 p.m.
Kate Buchanan Room, G
Lunch will be provided
Dear Lumberjacks,
As we prepare for the upcoming WSCUC review team visit, I’m excited to invite you to attend the Accreditation Site Visit Launch event—an opportunity to come together as a campus community, learn what to expect, and celebrate the work that brings us to this important moment.
On April 15–17, a WSCUC review team will visit campus as follow-up to our September submission of our 2025 Institutional Report. This visit marks an important milestone for our University and provides a meaningful opportunity to share who we are, how we serve our students, and where we’re headed as a polytechnic institution. It’s a chance to showcase the dedication, innovation, and care that define our campus.
At the launch event, we’ll outline what to expect during the April site visit, discuss why accreditation matters, and explore themes emerging from the report and the WSCUC Lines of Inquiry. The session will include interactive activities, opportunities for discussion, and prize drawings. I hope you’ll come ready to engage, ask questions, and help build enthusiasm as we prepare to welcome the review team.
I am truly proud of the work happening across our campus, and I hope you will join us as we build momentum toward April and continue shaping our shared future.
Looking forward to seeing you there!
Shawna Young
Interim Provost and Vice President for Academic Affairs
