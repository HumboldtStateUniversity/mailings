A Message from Humboldt State University

December 2, 2021

Phasing Out Email Accounts for Former and Inactive Campus Community Members

Humboldt State will begin phasing out inactive university accounts, including email, for former campus community members, including former students, alumni, and former employees. Accounts will start to close 6 months or later after theyâ€™re no longer associated with the University.
There are several reasons to discontinue old accounts:
  • The high cost to the University to maintain accounts for people no longer actively affiliated
  • Reducing confusion regarding people with the same name
  • Risk to the University of people no longer associated with the campus claiming to represent HSU
  • Very few of the accounts are regularly used
Faculty and staff are encouraged to ask graduating students for a personal email address in order to stay in contact, or encourage alumni to update their address to a non-humboldt.edu email in order to stay connected with the university.
Alumni are encouraged to sign up for Forever Humboldt with a personal email address. Forever Humboldt is the association of all Humboldt alumni, students, parents & families, faculty, and staff, intended to showcase pride in our university and encourage everyone to help support its mission. Learn more and sign up here.
General best practices:
  • Use your HSU email account for University business only
  • Maintain a personal account for utility bills, smartphone accounts, online subscriptions, etc.
