In an effort to keep campus green, the university is replacing paper evaluations with online evaluations for your classes. On the morning of April 22, you will receive emails from Course Evaluations by Students (CEbS@humboldt.edu) inviting you to anonymously evaluate your classes. The evaluations close on Friday, May 10 at 11:59 p.m. You will receive email reminders until you fill out the evaluation.

Your feedback to your instructors is important to faculty for improving teaching and course structure. These evaluations are also an important part of the retention, tenure and promotion process for your instructors. We encourage you to take the time to evaluate your instructors' teaching.

You can preview a sample evaluation and the email that you will receive at the Course Evaluations by Students (CEbS) website: https://www.humboldt.edu/cebs/. You can even take a practice evaluation and tell us what you think about the process.

Because the evaluations are online, you can fill them out from anywhere at any time you have computer access. Try it on your smartphone!

Thank you for helping us to keep HSU strong and provide the best possible learning opportunities.
