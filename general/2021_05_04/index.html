A Message from Humboldt State University

May 4, 2021

May Polytechnic Update

As the academic year comes to a close, we are grateful to our Polytechnic Working Groups that have spent the last several months closely examining our strengths and potential in regards to becoming California’s third Polytechnic University.

The working groups put in a great deal of effort consulting with the campus and larger community, hosting open forums, conducting surveys, and gathering information from all campus divisions. The working groups have submitted their drafts of the initial self study to us.

See a full list of the working groups and participants, as well as their charges, on the Polytechnic Self-Study website.

We are thankful to these groups for their hard work, creativity and leadership. They have engaged in a thorough, critical, and inclusive study of our university and the strengths we bring to a comprehensive polytechnic. We are excited to continue this work to articulate a polytechnic based on our collective purpose and shared values.

We will be working this summer to use what they submitted to develop our prospectus to the Chancellor’s Office. We will also be sharing information generated from the working groups via the Polytechnic Study website.

Thank you again for your hard work this semester exploring this exciting opportunity.

Sincerely,

Jenn Capps
Provost and Vice President for Academic Affairs
