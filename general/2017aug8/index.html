A Message from Humboldt State University

August 8, 2017

Loss of HSU Professor Michelle Lane

Dear University Community,

We are saddened to share news of the loss of Michelle Lane, an Associate Professor in Humboldt State Universityâ€™s School of Business. She was visiting with family when she died on August 7.

Her death is a great loss for our community, and we extend our condolences to her family, friends, and colleagues.

Michelle is remembered by colleagues and friends as being passionate about teaching and her students. She particularly enjoyed engaging students about strategy, quantitative analysis, and decision modeling classes, as well as incorporating sustainability at the undergraduate and MBA levels. Her interests and focus were on social responsibility, social entrepreneurship, and service learning.

Michelle is also remembered for being involved in her community. Every semester, she and her students worked on many course projects for local nonprofits. She had a great love for animals and was active in volunteering. She was on the Board of Directors for the Sequoia Humane Society.

Michelle is survived by three children and their spouses, three grandchildren, two siblings, and three dogs.

Students seeking additional support are encouraged to contact the Dean of Students office at 707.826.3504 or visit that office in Siemens Hall 211. Staff or faculty seeking additional support may utilize the Employee Assistance Program at 707.443.7358.
