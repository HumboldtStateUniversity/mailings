Thank you for registering for the CSU 2015 ATOD Educational Conference.

Check-in for the conference will be on April 16th from 9am-12pm in foyer on the second floor of the Marriot. We are delighted that the Monterey Bay Aquarium has provided us discounted rates in the form of coupons that decrease admission to Adults $27.95, Students/Senior $24.50, and Children $17.50. The coupon is good from April 15th-18th and covers up to six other people if you happen to have others traveling with you but not attending the conference. If you come in on Wednesday you can receive your coupon from the front desk staff, otherwise coupons will be distributed at check-in on Thursday.

We are excited to provide a special opportunity to the students in attendance. Students will be bussed to Cal State Monterey Bay at 4:30pm and back to the Marriot at 9pm. During the event we will provide dinner, and a groundbreaking, interactive, and accessible workshop using a community led, student centered approach to violence prevention.

Please note that no lunch will be provided on Friday. We encourage you to explore the Monterey Bay area and its lovely local businesses on the boardwalk and downtown area.

In representation of being a Green Campus, we have posted the descriptions of programs and the conference schedule on the website. This is done in attempt to be as green as possible. There will be notepads provided, printed with the outline of the schedule on the front.

Parking structures can be found here. We recommend using the lots titled "West" and "East" garage in blue. Details on cost can be found here.
