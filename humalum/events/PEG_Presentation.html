Please come and join us for a presentation of findings and interpretation of results for the HSU Alumni Attitude Survey, a study conducted last fall by the Performance Enhancement Group (PEG). This email-based survey was administered at 18 different CSU’s and HSU had over 800 alumni respondents. The results have really helped us understand the preferences of our alumni…and more specifically how they want to engage with the institution. Come join us for a one hour presentation by Rob Shoss, PEG’s senior researcher, on Thursday, March 26, 2009, at 4pm, with reception to follow at 5pm. Location: Nelson Hall East, Room #106.

The Alumni Attitude Study is a cooperative effort among institutions and PEG to measure and compare the values, preferences and perceptions that motivate alumni to engage with their alma mater. The Study has developed a reliable and cost effective survey process that provides Study participants with survey “comparables” and “benchmarks” to help assess institutional performance on alumni communication, development and programming efforts. More than 100 universities and associations have now surveyed their alumni through the Study.
