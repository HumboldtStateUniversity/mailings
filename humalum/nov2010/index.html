Hello Humboldt Alumni!

Awesomely fantastic? Fantastically awesome? We're running through the superlatives trying to best describe how much fun we had at HSU's Homecoming & Family Weekend last month.

Hundreds of folks turned up for the festivities. Campus tours and classes-without-quizzes were a big hit. Same goes for the pre-game tailgate and barbecue. The Jacks served up a big win over Dixie State (28-3) and everybody we talked to had a great time.

Photos and a video are at our Homecoming & Family Weekend Page »

Up next on our calendar is an alumni get-together in Berkeley.. If you're an alum in the Bay Area, join us at the Pyramid Alehouse, Brewery & Restaurant on Nov. 10 at 6:30 p.m. It's free for HSU alumni and $10 for guests. RSVP and get more information »

Humboldt History

Burling, or log rolling, is a perennial favorite of any Lumberjack Games. Taking a spin here on the North Coast means the possibility of dunking in the water when its 50 degrees out, but that's what makes our Lumberjacks so hearty.

See more historic photos in HSU's new history book by alum Katy M. Tahja. Visit our Alumni store to get your copy. »
