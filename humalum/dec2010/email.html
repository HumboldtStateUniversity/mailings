Congrats to Scholarship Recipients, and a little something for your smartphones

Congratulations to latest recipients of the HSU Alumni Association Scholarship, Daniel Birmingham and Sue-I-Chet Ray.

Daniel received his bachelor's degree in Sociology in 2010 and is currently working on his Certificate of Study in Geographic Information Systems. Daniel has been a dedicated member of the Y.E.S. House (Youth Educational Services) and even received the President's Volunteer Service Award for his work.

Sue-I-Chet is a first generation college student from Hoopa, Calif., and commutes an hour to Arcata everyday to work on her Social Work bachelor's degree. After graduating, she hopes to serve her community as a professional social worker.

We want to extend thanks to all the alumni who made this award possible. The Alumni Association Scholarship is a minimum $1,000 award funded by membership dues and the many generous donations alumni like you give to Humboldt State every year. It's also a group of dedicated alumni who review all the applications and select the winners—a big thanks to everybody involved and a really big thanks to Carol Kinser ('68) who chaired the selection committee.

Be on the look out for more events in your area. We've already got events planned for February in San Bernardino and San Diego.
