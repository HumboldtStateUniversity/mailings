Featured Video

Acres of strawberries might provide plenty of resources for honeybees and other natural pollinators, but what about when those crops are out of season? Planting diverse hedgerows to produce a year-round bloom can establish a permanent habitat for these helpful critters.

Diverse hedgerows that bloom year-round can really help out natural pollinators. This video features Rachael Morgan (’07 Wildland Soils), a Soil Conservationist for the Natural Resources Conservation Service in Butte County.
