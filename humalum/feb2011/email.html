Alumni scholarships; fighting fire with fire and more

Lots of students stop by our offices these days with their applications for our fall '11 Alumni Scholarship. Funded by folks like you, it's a great way to support today's students—and, we enjoy chatting with all the future alumni who drop in.

This semester Humboldt Alumni will be awarding two $1,000 scholarships. The award is for students related to an HSU alum, so download our PDF application and be sure to hand one to your nephew or kid sister—we're sure they wouldn't mind some help paying for another semester of books. Deadline is Friday, March 11.

And we couldn’t forget a big congrats to the previous winners of the Alumni Scholarship, Daniel Birmingham and Sue-I-Chet Ray.

This great scholarship exists because of the generous support of HSU alumni. Learn more about supporting Alumni Scholarships at HSU »
