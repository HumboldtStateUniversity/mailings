The Gift of Joy

The Humboldt State community is stepping up to make the season a little brighter for Humboldt County kids. Through Winter Wishes, staff, faculty, students, local alumni and community members are sponsoring nearly 100 local children in need and buying gifts off their wish list. Presents will be wrapped and delivered December 18. Thanks to all the sponsors!

Class Notes

Benjamin J. Crain, 2008 Biological Sciences, recently took a position as an Ecologist with the Smithsonian Environmental Research Center and the North American Center for Orchid Conservation. With a diverse group of collaborators, he conducts research in the Republic of Palau where they study the diversity and distribution of orchids, monitor their dynamics in the Ngardok Nature Reserve, and study the fundamental ecology of orchids by looking at their relationship with mycorrhizal fungi. The team’s goals are to improve knowledge and increase awareness of orchid diversity in Palau, understand the ecology of these plants, and develop strategic plans for conservation and restoration of orchid species and their habitats.
