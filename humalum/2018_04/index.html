Say Y.E.S. to Volunteering

Forever Humboldt invites HSU alumni to participate in Youth Educational Services’ volunteer days. The next opportunity is the Friends of the Dunes Volunteer Day on Saturday, April 14 from 9:30 a.m. to 12:30 p.m. at the Humboldt Coastal Nature Center. Help restore the dune ecosystem by removing invasive plants. This is a great way to both volunteer and stay connected to the HSU community.

Volunteer at Commencement

Saturday, May 12 is Commencement, and Forever Humboldt is seeking volunteers to help out in the Alumni Tent. We’ll be helping new graduates register with Forever Humboldt and selling HSU gear. If you’d like to help out, email forever@humboldt.edu or call 707.826.3132.

New Benefit

Based in San Francisco’s Golden Gate Park, the Academy is home to a world-class aquarium, planetarium, and natural history museum—all under one living roof.
