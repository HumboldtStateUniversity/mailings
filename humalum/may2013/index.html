Humboldt Honors

HSU celebrated its academic community with a full evening of events culminating in the Humboldt State Honors dinner. More than 20 students presented their research at a poster presentation before the main ceremonies began, which included the introduction of the 2013 Distinguished Alumni Award recipients. Distinguished Faculty were also recognized for their achievements in scholarship and teaching. Congratulations to all!
