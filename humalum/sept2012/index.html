Vote to Support Education!

For the first time ever, Humboldt State students are being asked to shoulder more of the costs of their education than the state: $51 million from students, $50 million from the state. And it could get even worse if voters reject the Governor’s tax measure (Proposition 30) in November and the “trigger” cuts slash another $5 million from HSU’s budget. Learn more about Proposition 30 and what it means for California at the California State Univeristy's website (PDF).

Be a Part of the Centennial Alumni Directory

HSU is preparing a centennial-year edition of the Humboldt State University Alumni Directory. It's being done in collaboration with Publishing Concepts, Inc. (PCI), a Dallas-based publisher of alumni directories.

Make sure you respond when PCI contacts you to ensure your information is up to date. You may also choose to opt out by requesting that your profile data not be included in the upcoming directory. Once published, the Directory will be exclusively available to Humboldt State alumni.

To learn more, visit Humboldt Alumni online.

Alumni Attitude Survey: Let Us Know What You Think

We want to learn more about your Humboldt State experience, how it influenced your life and the best way to communicate with you. Please join your fellow alumni in participating in an upcoming online alumni survey. Your input is essential and will guide our planning for the future.

Humboldt Alumni has commissioned Performance Enhancement Group, Ltd. of Houston to conduct the Alumni Attitude Study©. This study will measure alumni attitudes and how they impact participation with the association and Humboldt State University.

This is an online, confidential survey that will be sent to all HSU alumni with an e-mail address.

We value the input of all of our alumni and hope that you will participate in this endeavor with us. Thank you for your support and participation.

To learn more, visit Humboldt Alumni online.
