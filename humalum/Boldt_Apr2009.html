HSU Wins National Climate Award

Humboldt State University has been honored by the National Wildlife Federation as a winner in the annual competition Chill Out: Campus Solutions to Global Warming. The award honors U.S. schools that are advancing creative solutions to global warming on their campuses. HSU is one of eight winning schools that were chosen from a national pool of entries, and will be featured on a special webcast on April 15. More»
