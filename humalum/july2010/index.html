Thanks for the Ideas

Thanks to all of you who responded to our survey about the newsletter – and for including so many great comments about HSU. Based on your feedback, we've made a number of changes. For instance, we jazzed up the masthead, added a Humboldt-area image, and included more department-specific news. We're also working to include more photos and alumni profiles, and we'll be making other improvements in coming months. We always love hearing your questions and comments. Feel free to drop us a line anytime at alumni@humboldt.edu.
