Nominate a Distinguished Alum

Do you know an HSU alumnus or alumna who has made a major contribution to his or her community? You can help celebrate their good work by nominating them for a 2017 Distinguished Alumni Award.

Distinguished Alumni are individuals who are leaders in their fields or outstanding contributors to their community, nation, or Humboldt State University.

Award categories include:
The Distinguished Alumni Award,
The Distinguished Recent Alumni Award,
& The Distinguished Friend of the University Award.

Nominations are due to the Forever Humboldt Office by January 13, 2017 (Alumni & Engagement, HSU, 1 Harpst Street, Arcata, CA 95521 - or - Fax 707-826-5148).

HSU Bus in Eureka Trucker’s Parade—Join Us!

The annual Trucker’s Parade in Eureka is a Humboldt County holiday tradition. Forever Humboldt is looking for local alumni to help decorate and ride on HSU’s bus. The parade takes place Saturday, Dec. 10 at 6 p.m. with a decoration party at HSU on Friday afternoon. To join the fun, contact us at forever@humboldt.edu or 707-826-3132.
