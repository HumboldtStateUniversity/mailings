Legendary HSU Basketball Coach Dies

Alumni and community supporters gathered in Lumberjack Arena to remember Tom Wood, the longtime men's basketball coach who passed away in August. He was 68.

Sports writers say Wood built HSU's men's basketball program into one of the most consistent winners in the West region.

In memory of Wood, current men's head coach Steve Kinder has dedicated the 2016-17 season. Players will wear a black patch with 'TW463' signifying Wood's 463 career wins with the program. A scholarship fund is also being established in his and his wife’s honor.

Latest Class Notes

April Alexander (’15 Social Work, ‘16 Master’s of Social Work) is a clinical social worker at St. Joseph’s Hospital in Eureka. She recently presented at the Association of Community Cancer Center’s (ACCC) 33rd National Oncology Conference. Alexander’s presentation, “Rural Chemotherapy Clinic Student Volunteer Support Program,” was a program she designed and implemented in collaboration with the HSU Department of Social Work in the infusion clinic at St. Joseph Hospital’s Cancer Program to help patients undergoing chemotherapy treatment and to support the medical staff in the clinic.
