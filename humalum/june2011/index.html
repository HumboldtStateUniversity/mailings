Getting to Know You, Our Alumni

We're on a mission to get to know our alumni a little better.

To lead our efforts, we've launched the Alumni Discovery Project. The project gets current HSU students on the road and meeting with Alumni to share stories of their time at HSU, life after college and their passions and dreams. It's an exciting project and we know our Alumni will love relating their fond memories.

Seven students will be conducting interviews with a pool of alumni beginning this summer and we'll be sharing some of the stories we gather right here in the Humboldt Alumni newsletter. Stay tuned.
