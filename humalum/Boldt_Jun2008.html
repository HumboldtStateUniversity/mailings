Alumni Website Has Great New Features

HSU Alumni Relations has launched an all new website and online community. The community features an alumni directory, class notes, social networking capabilities, classifieds, and a career network.

Career Network - The Career Network is a powerful tool for alumni who are looking for employees and alumni who are looking for a career. A free service, we invite all alumni to sign-up today and help build the foundation of a powerful tool for all Humboldt alumni.

Check out the new site. Use of the community features is for alumni and is free. To register, simply click on "First Time Login" and use the constituent ID from your Boldt announcement email and get started today.
