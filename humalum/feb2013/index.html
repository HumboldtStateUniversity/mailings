Update Your Contact Info

HSU is preparing a centennial year edition of the Humboldt State University Alumni Directory. It's being done in collaboration with Publishing Concepts, Inc. (PCI), a Dallas-based publisher of alumni directories.

Make sure you respond when PCI contacts you to ensure your data is correct. Once published, the Directory will be exclusively available to Humboldt State alumni in both printed and CD-ROM format.

If you have any questions, contact Humboldt State Alumni at (707) 826-3132 or alumni@humboldt.edu

Volunteer Opportunity

Humboldt State University is seeking alumni interested in joining the Alumni Ambassador program. Alumni Ambassadors help recruit students at college fairs, have one-on-one talks with potential students or attend Humboldt State receptions throughout the state. You can learn more by visiting http://alumni.humboldt.edu/volunteer or by emailing alumni@humboldt.edu.
