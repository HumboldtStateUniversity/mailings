Finals Pizza Party

Last year, David, a student who had nearly 2,000 J points left at the end of semester, bought a stack of pizzas and treated campus to an impromptu pizza party. This year, Forever Humboldt, with the help of alumni supporters, brought the party back to life - handing out slices to hundreds of students during
Finals Week.

To help ease the stress of finals, various campus groups also brought dogs to campus for “puppy therapy” and the library offered guided meditation sessions.

Results: Graduation Pledge Survey

More than 120 alumni recently took the time to answer a Graduation Pledge Survey and helped HSU gain valuable insights on how people are "living the pledge." More than 60 percent said they were fulfilling the pledge or planned to do so in the future. Community involvement, sustainable purchases, activism, and charity donations were the most common ways. Thank you to everyone who participated!

Latest Class Notes

Stefanie Lauren Kalin, 2010 Psychology, is a licensed marriage and family therapist. She has developed her clinical experiences providing mental health services to a range of patients. She specializes in mind-body connection and trauma residency with training in eye movement desensitization and reprocessing (EMDR). Kalin met her husband, Rory, in undergrad at HSU and became Mr. and Mrs. Kalin in 2015. They returned to Humboldt in 2017 to begin careers serving Humboldt County. She will be practicing as a mental health clinician for Humboldt County Department of Health and Human Services.

Gary Banuelos, 1979 German, was recently selected as “USDA Pac West Senior Scientist of the Year" for his work on using phytoremediation to clean-up contaminated soils. In 1992, he was selected as "USDA Early Career Scientist of the Year." After earning his doctorate in plant nutrition from Hohenhiem/Tuebingen Universities in Germany, he worked on developing "green technologies" for remediating contaminated soils and waters worldwide. In addition, he lectures at the University of Zürich in Switzerland, University of Santiago in Chile and at University of Science and Technology of China.
