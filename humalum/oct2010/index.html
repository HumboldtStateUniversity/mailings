Hello Humboldt Alumni!

We've been on the road lately, and it's been fun meeting up with so many proud alumni in Long Beach, Seattle and Portland. We've enjoyed hearing about your great experiences at HSU.


Check out more photos on our Facebook page »

In Portland and Seattle we screened the National Geographic documentary, Climbing Redwood Giants, which features HSU forestry professor Steve Sillett. It was was a big hit.

Meanwhile, alumni gathered for a beer tasting event at Belmont Brewing Company in Long Beach. It was also a big success, and thanks to everyone who joined us.

The big event this month is Homecoming & Family Weekend. With only one week to go, time's ticking, but you can still register now.

Insider's tip—this year we're rolling out our first-ever Arts Showcase as part the weekend's activities. Here's a sneak peak of the Art happenings so far:

  • Live mural painting by Art Students
  • Improv dance performances
  • Theatre performances and Short Film screenings
  • Jazz combo

And make sure to be at the football game Saturday evening. The team is on a serious role this year, beating powerhouse Central Washington 25-24.

More is being planned, even now, so mark your calendars and join us on Oct. 15 - 17!

If you already purchased one, thank you! We'd love to have to you stop by the Redwood Bowl Plaza and see your newly installed brick.

All proceeds from the sale of bricks supports either HSU Athletics or Alumni Association Scholarship Endowments—programs that directly help students at Humboldt State.
