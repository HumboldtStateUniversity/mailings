Alumni Art on Display

The Reese Bullen Gallery presents its first in-person exhibition of the 2021-22 year: Selections from the HSU Permanent Collection Purchase Prize works. Generously funded by the Office of the President, this exhibition selects from a backlog of student purchases dating back to 1977. This exhibition shows the ongoing creative endeavors of our past alumni through this small thread of the Purchase Prize.

To learn more about the galleries at HSU, go to art.humboldt.edu/galleries  »

Donor Honors Roots by Helping to Launch HSU Food Sovereignty Lab

With a personal interest in social justice and food, Leah B. Thibault, whose family has deep ties to HSU, gave $50,000 in endowed funds to support student interns and research assistants in the Food Sovereignty Lab & Cultural Workshop Space. Another $10,000 will go towards seed funds to launch the lab and internship program.

Latest Class Notes

Ryan Ward (‘17, History)
After graduating from Humboldt State, Ryan obtained a teaching credential from San Jose State University and was hired as a Government and Economics teacher at Westmont High School. After teaching two classes for the 2020-21 academic year, he has been hired to a full-time position for the 2021-22 school year.

Aydee Zielke (‘08, Environmental Science and Management)
Aydee recently obtained her dream job, joining the San Dieguito River Park JPA, taking on the role as the park environmental planner. Her work includes habitat conservation, preservation, planning, permitting, and mitigation for trails and parkland. She is also using her cartography skills to create map exhibits, maintain, and update GIS data. She had worked for various private consulting firms and earned a Master of Urban and Regional Planning from the University of Hawaii (2015). She met her husband while studying at Humboldt and they have more recently taken on the role of parents, raising their two outgoing toddlers in Coastal North County San Diego.

Gloria Miranda (‘08, Art)
Gloria’s most recent accomplishment is that she is now a recent graduate from Claremont Graduate University Master of Science in Information Systems Technology in Data Science as well as a Master of Business for the Arts through Sotheby’s Institute of Art at Claremont Graduate University.
