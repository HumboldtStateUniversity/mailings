Nominate a Distinguished Alumnus

Humboldt State University  has been recognizing the achievements of alumni since 1960 when it launched its annual Who’s Who Award.

We are now accepting nominations for the 2010 Distinguished Alumni Awards. Distinguished alumni are individuals recognized as leaders in their fields, or outstanding contributors to their community, nation, or Humboldt State University.

Distinguished Alumni Awards include: The Distinguished Alumni Award, The Distinguished Recent Alumni Award, and The Distinguished Friend of the University Award.

Click here to nominate a distinguished alumnus. Nominations are due by November 15.

Save the Date! The 2010 Distinguished Alumni Awards banquet will be held April 23, 2010.
