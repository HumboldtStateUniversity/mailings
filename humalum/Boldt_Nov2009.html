Alumni and Friends Give $3.5 Million to HSU

Humboldt State University received more than $3.5 million from almost 8,300 donors in the last fiscal year. HSU ranked number one in alumni donors among its CSU peer group, with 3,880 alumni donors. Your gifts supported the new Library Café, new Smart Classroom technology, equipment for the Behavioral and Social Sciences Building, student scholarships, and much more. Thank you! More»

This year's Homecoming & Family Weekend was a great success. Hundreds of people travelled to HSU to watch the Jacks triumph over Southern Oregon, take in a campus tour, enjoy reminiscing with old friends, catch up with professors and check out the 2009 version of the student experience. More»

 

Unraveling the Moon Tree Mystery

Did you know that HSU is home to “moon trees”? The trees were germinated from seeds that traveled in Apollo 14 and circled the moon. Humboldt State received a collection of the seedlings in 1976, and they were planted all over campus (download the PDF map showing the trees' general locations). More»

Humboldt State military veterans say the thing they miss most about their service experience—soldierly camaraderie—is supplied in spades by the University’s year-old Veterans Enrollment and Transition Services Office, staffed by fellow vet undergraduate and graduate students. More»
