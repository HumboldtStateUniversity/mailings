Hello Humboldt Alumni – and a big congrats to our thousands of
new alumni!

More than 2,100 students joined the ranks of the HSU alumni at this year's Commencement ceremonies. A big thanks to all the volunteers on hand in the Alumni tent for helping to welcome the newbies! We chatted with plenty of proud graduates, families and friends. The fog/clouds/mist stuck around all day, so sweatshirts and coffee mugs were definitely popular souvenirs (to get your own, check out our store).

See the Commencement slideshow. Find out about the Outstanding Students of the Year. Read excerpts from the keynote address in the SF Chronicle.

Some of the latest news from HSU…

The University proudly honored four alumni at the Distinguished Alumni Awards in April. Awardees shared some of their Humboldt experiences, and spent time with students earlier in the day. Read more and see video highlights.

Princeton Review is saying what we've known for years – that HSU is a greener-than-green, eco-conscious destination for students who care about sustainability. Read more »

A new CSU report pegs Humboldt State's annual economic impact on the North Coast at $190 million. Another biggie: the 23-campus CSU system generates $5.43 for the economy for every $1 invested by the state. Read more or see the study

An HSU professor and student are warning of serious fire threats due to sudden oak death, a non-native disease that infects tanoak. The research appeared in the journal Forest Ecology and Management.
Read more »

If you're in the habit of planning early, mark your calendars for Oct. 15-17 for Homecoming & Family Weekend. It's bigger and better this year, with events like a new arts showcase that includes art exhibits and musical and theater performances, an expanded tailgate and BBQ, and, yes, a football game.
