Party on the Plaza Kicks off Centennial Year

Humboldt State University’s yearlong Centennial Celebration kicked off Saturday, Aug. 24, with a 100th Birthday Festival on the Arcata Plaza. The event was hosted by the Arcata Chamber of Commerce, Arcata Main Street and the City of Arcata, and featured tons of old-fashion fun.

Thousands of alumni, students, parents and community members turned out for the afternoon party and our photographer captured all the fun.

See all the photos from the event at Flickr.

And get all the details for the Centennial Year, including a full list of activities, at http://www.humboldt.edu/100

Be Part of the HSU Human 100 Photo

Fresno State got together to celebrate its 100th birthday in 2011. Humboldt State is also celebrating its 100th year with a massive Human 100 photograph. The photo shoot is set for Friday, Aug. 30 at 3 p.m. on the campus Events Field (or, if you attended Humboldt State College, it will be where the old baseball field was).

There will be free t-shirts for the first 800 participants, so be sure to show up early.

Be sure to register for a Centennial Birthday Roadshow in your area. There are already more than 20 scheduled across the country.
