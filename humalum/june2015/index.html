Forever Humboldt Students Support Classmates with Book Donation

Student club members of Forever Humboldt are easing the financial burden for current students. They recently donated a set of textbooks to the University Library, which were purchased with funds donate by current students. It’s a great example of students giving back.

According to the library, textbooks like these were checked out 25,000 times last year, and hundreds of students benefited from having them available.

To learn more about the Forever Humboldt Students Club, visit http://forever.humboldt.edu/students »

HSU Partners with Stargazer Barn Winery

Humboldt County’s Stargazer Barn winery has introduced two new wines that will help support Humboldt State University.

Lumberjack Red and Lumberjack White are special, limited production blends made entirely of Humboldt-grown grapes. They are now available at Wildberries in Arcata and online at the Stargazer Barn website.

A portion of proceeds from every bottle sold will be given to the Humboldt Loyalty Fund, which supports the student experience at HSU with laboratory equipment, new technology, special projects, and more.

CSU Reaches 3 Million Alumni

Humboldt State’s class of 2015 is part of the 3 million living alumni of the California State University system. And those alumni make a big difference for California: One in 10 employees in California holds a degree from the CSU, and every year, the CSU returns nearly $4.53 to the state for every $1 invested. Tyler Cline, 2009 History, pursued his Master of Arts in Public History from Sacramento State, graduating in 2011. While pursuing his M.A., Cline worked as a processing archivist at the California State Archives. He then worked as an archivist in Anchorage, Alaska, with the U.S. National Parks Service. In 2013, Cline accepted a tenure-track position with the University of Wyoming American Heritage Center as the Digital Programs Archivist, where he is responsible for preservation born-digital collections, and oversees the department of six engaged in mass-digitization of collection material for access on the web.
