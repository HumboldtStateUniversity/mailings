Catch HSU Football Action

Football fever is heating up at Humboldt State. The Jacks are in full swing preparing for the first game against Mesa State in Grand Junction, Colo., on Sept. 1.

The first home game is set for Sept. 8. Lumberjack fans across the nation can tune into the CBS Sports Network for live coverage when the Jacks head into one of the toughest games of the season against Central Washington.

If you're planning on watching the game, contact Humboldt Alumni for a free Watch Party Kit.

On Oct. 1, join HSU for the Homecoming & Family Weekend game as the Jacks try for a repeat of last year’s homecoming victory over Dixie State.

And for our all our alumni in the Sacramento area, don your Humboldt State gear and join fellow HSU alumni for a pre-game tailgate when the Jacks take on UC Davis on Oct. 8.

Alumni Welcome New HSU Students

HSU kicked off the fall semester last week, following a busy weekend of students moving into campus housing.

A big group of alumni supporters came out to lend a hand with moving and offer words of advice to the incoming freshman class. Thanks to all of the alumni who came out for Freshman Move-in Day! It was a big success and we can’t wait to do it again next year.
