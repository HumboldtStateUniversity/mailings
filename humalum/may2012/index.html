Recent Happenings

HSU thanks all the alumni who helped reach out to high school students in Southern California last month. They visited the schools to talk about why HSU is a great place to go to college—often times with students who’ve never even heard of Humboldt State.

Lynn Huynh (’11, Theatre Arts) and Robert Chacon (’93, English) took part in the 18th annual Adelante Mujer Latina Conference and Tiffany Newton (’08, Journalism) and Daniel Bush (’08, Pre-Physical Therapy) participated in the Moorpark High School college fair. Edward Colacion (’81, Natural Resource Planning) gave a talk to Upward Bound high school students in Boyle Heights about the opportunities HSU could offer. Upward Bound is a federal TRIO program that assists low income, first generation students with access into the college/university of their choice. Thank you!

If you’re interested in volunteering for HSU, email alumni@humboldt.edu and we’ll let you know how you can help.
