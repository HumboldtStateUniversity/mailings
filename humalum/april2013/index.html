Share Your Stories & Photos

Have some great photos from your Humboldt State days? Maybe a memorable story about someone who inspired you or an event that surprised you? HSU’s Centennial offers a great time to share.

The HSU community has contributed to a growing historical slideshow, which is featured at the Centennial webpage, humboldt.edu/100/share. Your photos are welcomed too, and can be submitted online. Photo prints are welcomed at:

Humboldt Alumni
Humboldt State University
1 Harpst St. Arcata, CA 95521

Submitted stories will be edited and featured for one month during the Centennial Celebration. Highlight publications include the Alumni Newsletter, Humboldt magazine and elsewhere.

Volunteer Opportunity

Humboldt State University is seeking alumni interested in joining the Alumni Ambassador program. Alumni Ambassadors help recruit students at college fairs, have one-on-one talks with potential students or attend Humboldt State receptions throughout the state. You can learn more by visiting http://alumni.humboldt.edu/volunteer by emailing alumni@humboldt.edu.
