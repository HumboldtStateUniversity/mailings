Alumni Owned Business Highlight
The Refinish Line  »

The Refinish Line is a Texas-based furniture restoration business owned and operated by alum Christopher Matthews (‘75, Business Administration). Christopher has always had an interest in wood, in particular contrasting wood grains and colors. For many years he refinished his own furniture, so when he retired from his career as a software system's engineer, he decided to see if he could sell his services to the public. This afforded him the opportunity to work with many more wood species, refinishing products, and refinishing techniques. And, it worked! The Refinish Line will offer all California State University alumni a 15% discount and all Humboldt alumni a 20% discount.

Shannon Barger (‘14, Kinesiology & Recreation Administration)
After six years working at NASA’s Jet Propulsion Laboratory, Shannon has gone back to get her Masters in Education from the University of Washington. Additionally, she’ll be earning her graduate certificate in Education for the Environment and Community at Islandwood on Bainbridge Island, WA.

Christopher Rush (‘74, Natural Resources)
Christopher Rush completed hiking the Pacific Crest Trail Mexico to Canada in 2016 after hiking it in sections post retirement. After working the first half of his career with USDA & USDI (USFS-Willamette NF & BLM-California Desert), he spent the last half with the Environmental Office at Edwards Air Force Base in California where Rush retired as Chief of the Conservation Branch in 2005. Married to alumna Carol (Kohlenberger) Rush (‘74, Natural Resources).
