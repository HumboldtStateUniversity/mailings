Thank you to the hundreds of alumni who participated in our alumni attitude survey. We appreciate you taking the time to let us know how you feel about Humboldt State and how we can better meet your needs.

 

Q: Why does the Alumni Association have affinity programs?

A: An affinity program is an agreement between the University and a partner to offer discounted services to alumni in areas such as travel, insurance, or credit cards.  In addition to discounts for alumni, these affinity partners give a portion of their business back to your Alumni Association in support of HSU, while also providing sponsorship opportunities for other alumni events and programs.

Q: Can I opt-out of receiving mailings and special offers from affinity programs?

A: Yes. We have a form that you can complete online. (Note: you may receive offers for up to two months after you have completed the form.) http://alumni.humboldt.edu/privacy

 

The Alumni Association is proud to feature our partnership with Marsh Affinity Group Services, which provides you with access to several programs, including:

* Level Term Life Insurance
* Major Medical
* Catastrophe Major Medical
* Short Term Medical
* Long Term Care
