A New Semester, Alumni Events in San Bernardino and San Diego

On campus, we're excited to welcome back the students after winter break. The spring semester has lots to offer. For alumni in the Humboldt area, CenterArts has great performances lined up and Jacks' Basketball is featured in Lumberjack Arena through February.

For our alumni in San Diego and California's Inland Empire, we'd love to catch up. At 6:30 p.m., Wednesday, Feb. 2, we'll be in San Diego, screening the National Geographic film Climbing Redwood Giants featuring HSU Forestry Professor Steve Sillett, at the Rock Bottom Restaurant. Then, on Feb. 3, we're hosting an alumni social at BJ's Brewhouse in San Bernardino. Hope we see you there! For more details, visit our website.

Want Humboldt Alumni events in your area? Email us at alumni@humboldt.edu.
