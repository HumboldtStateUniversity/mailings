Who is a Golden Graduate?

Golden Graduates are those alumni that graduated 50 years ago or more from Humboldt State.

The Humboldt State University Alumni Association is proud to honor a special group of alumni at this year’s Golden Grad Plus reunion. On this occasion, we will recognize and welcome the Classes 1956 and 1957 into the ranks of Golden Graduates.

What is Golden Grad Plus?

The Humboldt State University Alumni Association would also like to recognize our returning Golden Graduates from classes prior to 1956. We are calling this reunion “Golden Grad Plus” to reflect the inclusion of all Golden Graduates.

You will not want to miss this once-in-a-lifetime event to celebrate the golden anniversary of the Classes of 1956 and 1957 and visit with friends and classmates. Spouses and guests are welcome!

If you graduated in 1957 or earlier, look for your invitation to come in the mail. You may also register online.
