New Online Community Features

The Humboldt State University Alumni Association is proud to announce the roll out of two new features on our Online Community – the Career Network and the Business Card Swap.  These new features, are being added to satisfy the requests of countless alums.

With the Career Network, an alum will be able to post their resume, search the job posting listings, and check out the company profiles. Take advantage of the Career Network provided by the HSU Alumni Association. "Let your resume work for you." Business owners and managers can post jobs, for free.

Our Business Card Swap program will enable you to promote your business and network with other alumni. If you're moving to a new area and looking for a realtor or need some help preparing your taxes, this is a great resource to find what you need!

The HSUAA is proud to help alums support each other and make valuable career connections!

If you aren't already a member of the online community, join now. It is free and easy. As a member you will enjoy access to popular features such as the Alumni Directory and Class Notes. Look for the Career Network and Business Card Swap features to go live late September.

Humboldt Alumni Wine Club is Here

The Humboldt Alumni Wine Club is up and running and ready for you to join.  Join the Humboldt Alumni Wine Club today, and receive unique artisan wines from wineries in which a Humboldt State alum is involved. Each shipment will feature premium, award-winning wines delivered right to your doorstep.  Here is a link to the site for the wine club: http://www.humboldtalumniwineclub.com/.

We are really excited to offer this wine club to our alums.  It gives you a chance to receive some great wine and to support the good work of other HSU alumni.  Initially, club members will get some excellent wine, which may or may not be produced by a winery with an HSU connection -- until we have developed a good list of alum-related vintners.  If you know of someone to add to the list, have them visit the site (see link above) and sign them up.


HSU Alumni Ambassador Program Update

Greetings! The fall 2007 semester is underway and I want to extend my appreciation to all Alumni Ambassadors for your assistance with our recruitment activities over the past year. There are currently fifty volunteers who attended college fairs and receptions, participated in our online chats, gave presentations to high school students and assisted prospective students through the admissions process. This fall we are enrolling our largest freshman class in the history of Humboldt State University and each ambassador can take some credit for this amazing accomplishment.

Activities to recruit the next freshman class for fall 2008 have already begun. If you are interested in signing up to participate as an Alumni Ambassador for the coming year, contact me at geldin@humboldt.edu or through our web site, at http://www.humboldt.edu/alumreps/ . The event calendar for fall has been posted and I am always looking for ambassadors to assist with our ‘adopt a high school’ program or speak individually with prospective students about their Humboldt State experiences. This past summer, several ambassadors came by to visit while vacationing in the area. They left with our latest materials, pennants and posters and will share the information with students and families in their communities. The photo above is of Dennis Perez (’91) who lives with his family in Temecula. Dennis, who came to visit HSU last month, is a teacher at Chaparral High School and proudly recruits prospective students to HSU as an Alumni Ambassador. If you, or someone you know, are interested in having materials mailed to you, please contact me.

Thank you again for your ongoing dedication and commitment to this program. I look forward to working with you in the coming year!

Best Wishes,
Rhonda Geldin
Assistant Director of Admissions
geldin@humboldt.edu
