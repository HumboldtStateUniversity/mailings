HSU Unveils "Greenest" Building in CSU

You can’t miss the latest addition to Humboldt State’s campus—all five stories and 84,000 square feet of it. The new Behavioral and Social Sciences (or “BSS”) building opened its doors this fall and will provide students, faculty, and staff with much-needed elbow room. Alumni are invited to attend the ribbon-cutting ceremony during Homecoming 2007.

Paddlefest Celebrates Five Years of Success

The Humboldt Bay Paddlefest, an event conceived by the Humboldt State University Alumni Association, is celebrating its 5th year of success this fall on September 29 and 30.  A premier West Coast paddling event for all skill levels, Paddlefest draws paddling enthusiasts from as far away as Seattle, Portland, Redding, and the Bay Area.
