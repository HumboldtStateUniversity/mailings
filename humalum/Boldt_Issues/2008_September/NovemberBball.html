Disney Classic Tournament

November 7-9, 2008

Humboldt State men’s basketball will experience its own November Madness in Southern California. The Lumberjacks will bring their game. They need you to attend and cheer them on to victory!

Games will be played at the Anaheim Convention Center.

Make your plans to attend today! Bring the entire family and make a weekend of it!

Game Schedule
Fri, Nov 7 vs. Fort Lewis College at 2:30 p.m.
Sat, Nov 8 vs. Southwest Minnesota State at 5 p.m.
Sun, Nov 9 vs. Alaska Anchorage at 1:30 p.m.

Packages
Packages that include hotel accommodations, park passes, and commemorative programs are available.
Read more about it.»
Book it!» (Make sure to put HUMBOLDT as your College Name!)

Game Tickets
Tournament tickets are available through Ticketmaster beginning October 1, 2008.

Additional LA area activities are listed below.

 

Alumni Reception at Disney Classic

November 8, 2008
3-5 p.m.

Prior to the game, HSU Athletics and Alumni Relations will be hosting a reception at the Anaheim Convention Center, from 2:30-5 p.m. (More information will follow.)  The reception will include appetizers.   We are asking for an RSVP so that we know how many people to expect, so please include the number of people in your party. RSVP on our website (or alumni@humboldt.edu or 707-826-3132.)

 

Alumni Gathering at the Getty Center

November 8, 2008
11 a.m.

Join your fellow Humboldt State alumni for a private tour of the Getty Collections. A no-host lunch will follow. RSVP's required. RSVP on our website (or alumni@humboldt.edu or 707-826-3132.)

Admission and Parking
Admission to the Getty Center is FREE—no tickets or reservations required for general admission. Parking is $10 per car. For more parking information, see Hours, Directions, Parking and frequently asked questions.
