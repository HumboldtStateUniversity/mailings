Is your thesis/bound project in Humboldt Digital Scholar?

Is your thesis or bound project in the HSU Library’s collection?  If so, do you have an electronic copy, preferably PDF?  If the answer to both questions is yes, and if your thesis/bound project is not already in Humboldt Digital Scholar, we’d like to hear from you.

A service of the HSU Library, Humboldt Digital Scholar (HDS) is a non-commercial, open access, online archive for HSU faculty and staff scholarship and for faculty-sponsored student scholarship, including Masters theses and bound projects.  Items in HDS are permanently preserved and can be viewed by scholars and others world-wide. 

HSU theses are in high demand, both here and through interlibrary loan.  Submission to HDS has been an option for graduate students since 2005, and it is not unusual for us to hear from someone delighted to find just the thesis they were looking for online.  We also receive requests to add theses that are not yet in HDS—yours could be among them.

If you would like to add your electronic  thesis/bound project to HDS, please contact us at dscholar@humboldt.edu.


Submitted by Humboldt Digital Scholar Steering Committee
