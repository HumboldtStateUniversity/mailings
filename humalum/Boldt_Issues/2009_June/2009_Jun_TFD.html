Alumni Reunion

Alumni of the Theatre, Film and Dance Department are having a reunion, July 11-12, 2009, to honor the memory of Dick Rothrock and raise funds for the Richard R. Rothrock Memorial Endowment. Rothrock was active in the theatre department from 1961 until his death in 1992.

Friday, July 10, 2009

Those arriving on Friday, July 10, can find their friends at a no host "Meet and Greet" at the Days Inn, Valley West, Arcata.

Saturday, July ll, 2009

Saturday at 9:30 gather in the lobby of John Van Duzer (formerly Sequoia) Theatre for coffee, and reconnections followed by a tour of theatre facilities and campus. Then check-out your favorite beach or the Farmers Market on the Plaza...one of the oldest in the state. At 2 PM festivities continue in JVD theatre. Saturday evening, 7:30, world class performers, James Donlon and Yass Hakoshima will give a mime/movement performance on the main stage. The public is invited and ticket sales from this event will go to the Rothrock Memorial Endowment.

Sunday, July 12, 2009

Sunday at 9:00, join friends for breakfast at the Samoa Cookhouse. Afternoon plans are underway for a baseball game (BYOBall and mitt) and/or wine tasting and bocce ball at the Fieldbrook Winery. In the evening you can catch a hilarious piece of original theatre at Dell Arte's outdoor venue in Blue Lake (www.dellarte.com).

For further information and updates contact Kate Kane: kikalanikane@yahoo.com
Please get in touch with all your friends from Humboldt years, urge them to attend, and get on Kate's List. A reunion is only as much fun as you make it!

Janice Rothrock
HSC class of '64
