HSUAA-Wildlife Chapter Annual Meeting

Wildlife Chapter of HSU Alumni Association is holding their annual meeting in conjunction with the Western Section of The Wildlife Society in Visalia, Calif. on January 29, 2010, from 7:30 to 8:30 AM. The conference location is at the Visalia Convention Center with the Room location to be determined. Detailed information on the room assignment will be available at the conference registration desk. Food service will be available at the meeting and coffee will be provided to all early risers.

Business agenda items include an update from Wildlife Faculty on current events and activities within the Department. Numerous scholarship awards and recognition have been earned by a growing contingent of students. The Wildlife program has experienced continued growth even with recent economic conditions. Group Travel plans and a report from the Mossman safari to Zambia will entertain and promote good fellowship for all who can muster the energy to join us for the early morning meeting.

Visalia's Convention Center is located at 303 East Acequia which can be accessed by taking the Court St. (also designated Hwy 63) from Hwy 198 east of Hwy 99 in Visalia, CA 93291, telephone (559) 713-4000.

Accommodations are available in the Marriott within the convention center or at Comfort Suites and Hamptoon Inn nearby.
