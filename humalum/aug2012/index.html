What does Humboldt State mean to you? Share Here.

For radio DJ and alum Mike Dronkers (’99, Journalism) HSU is like a "sandbox." "You can get in, you can get dirty, it’s kind of fun, you can make mistakes, nobody gets hurt, but at the end of the day, you learn what works and what doesn’t," he says.

Dronkers shared his connection in a new project called “Humboldt in a Word.” It’s an effort to capture the connection that friends, students, staff, faculty, and alumni share with Humboldt State. Hundreds of others have already share their word. Now it’s your turn. Share your connection to HSU and the one word that captures that spirit. Text and video submissions are welcome at humboldt.edu/oneword.

Volunteer Opportunity of the Month

Humboldt State University is seeking alumni interested in joining the Alumni Ambassador program. Just fill out a quick questionnaire and staff from Humboldt State Admissions will pair you with an event to match your interests. Alumni Ambassadors help recruit students at college fairs, have one-on-one talks with potential students or attend Humboldt State receptions throughout the state. For more information, visit alumni.humboldt.edu/page.aspx?pid=312 or email alumni@humboldt.edu.
