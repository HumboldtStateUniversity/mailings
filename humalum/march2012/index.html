Humboldt Magazine, the Ultimate Sport and More

The spring edition of Humboldt magazine is out. This newest edition includes lots of class notes from our alumni from 1950 to today, a look back at Humboldt from the '30s to the '60s, the ultimate story on Ultimate Disc, and a lot more.

If you have ideas for something we should cover in the magazine, let us know at alumni@humboldt.edu.

Anthropologist Alum Uncovers Primate’s Ultrasonic ‘Speech’

Research by Humboldt State anthropology lecturer and alumna Marissa Ramsier (’03 Anthropology) found that tarsiers, one of the world’s smallest primates, probably has an ultrasound warning system. Read more on Wired.com »

Recent Happenings

San Diego sunshine and Humboldt spirit! Nearly 150 alumni, parents and friends got together recently at Scripps' Birch Aquarium. The night was capped off by a panel discussion featuring three HSU alumni: Stephen Hillenburg (creator of SpongeBob SquarePants), Bruce Appelgate (Director of Field Operations at Scripps) and Craig Heberer (biologist with the National Marine Fisheries Service). Alumni Profile

Alumni in Jamaica

Volunteer Opportunities

Help with Scholarships

Humboldt Alumni is seeking volunteers to review scholarship applications and help select next fall's Alumni Association scholarship award recipients.

No meetings are required, just access to email and a willingness to review applications. If you're interested, please email Kim Sekas by March 21.

Speed Networking in Humboldt

Alumni volunteers in the Humboldt area are needed to participate in a Speed Networking mixer in Arcata on Tuesday, April 26.

Students will learn how to pitch themselves and make a good first impression. Alumni can talk about their post-college job experience and provide possible employer or industry contacts. Everyone will have some fun! If you're interested, please email Kim Sekas at Humboldt Alumni.

HSU University Center Board of Directors

The University Center is looking for a Humboldt-area alum to join its board of directors. Monthly meetings are held on campus and board members serve a two year term. For more information, email Dean Hart, Director of Alumni Relations.
