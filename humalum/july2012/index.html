Rowing Wins First-Ever National Championship

The HSU Rowing team recently won its first-ever NCAA Division II National Championship, ending a seven-year run by rival Western Washington University. “There was no way we were going to get second. It just wasn't going to happen," said senior rower Chyna Balonick (’12, Kinesiology). After the victory, head coach and HSU alumna Robin Meiggs (’91, Physical Education) earned a second consecutive NCAA Division II Coach of the Year award. Read more rowing coverage at HSUjacks.com »

Humboldt Hags Finish 'Ultimate' Season

The Humboldt Hags— HSU’s women's Ultimate Disc team—finished a great season with a No. 8 ranking by USA Ultimate, a second place finish in regionals and a tie for 17th at the national level. The team was profiled in a recent edition of SkyD, a magazine dedicated to competitive ultimate disc. Read the story »
