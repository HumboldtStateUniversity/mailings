Hello Humboldt Alumni!

We've updated your newsletter and will be sending it monthly. Please take a few minutes to check it out and catch up with your alma mater. And as always, we'd like to hear your thoughts, ideas or complaints - alumni@humboldt.edu or 707.826.3132.

Some of the latest good news…

Top HSU honors go to four seriously successful alumni. They'll be recognized at the April 23 Distinguished Alumni Awards Banquet and will also have a chance to speak with classes earlier in the day. As a bonus, the event is at the world's coolest Victorian, the Carson Mansion in Eureka. Get your tickets now »

Three alumni and grad student Becki Menten just helped the North Coast get $4.4 million in stimulus funding for clean energy projects. Read More »

A big shout-out to the women's and men's basketball teams which both made it to the NCAA Division II postseason. It was also men's coach Tom Wood's final season – he retires after 29 years and 463 wins. Women's Team News » | Men's Team News »

Speaking of athletics, the community recently raised $174,000 for athletics scholarships at the annual dinner and auction. Read More »

And if the end of winter has you thinking day-trips and vacations, check out the new "geo-tourism" website highlighting the Redwood Coast. Geography Professor Joy Adams and her students helped build it. Read More »

Your gifts support HSU students

Humboldt Alumni are big supporters of HSU students and programs, and your gifts make a real difference. During the last year, your gifts through the Annual Fund have supported scholarships, classroom technology upgrades, new lab equipment and more. Thank you!
