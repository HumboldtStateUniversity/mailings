Stay Connected to HSU – New Graduate Special for Joining Humboldt Alumni!

Humboldt Alumni is proud to recognize the Graduating Class of 2010…its accomplishments and its dreams! Humboldt Alumni wishes you well in your future endeavors, and welcomes you to stay connected with both your friends and Humboldt State for a lifetime.

New Graduate memberships are being offered at special discounted rate of $20! That's a savings of $15 off the regular annual fee of $35. Please come by our office in Siemens #215 or visit our booth at Grad Fair on May 24 or 25 to sign up. The New Grad rate is good through June 30, 2010.

Let Humboldt Alumni help you stay in touch with everyone in your Humboldt family -- friends, classmates, and faculty. When you join HSUAA you can:

  • Keep your humboldt.edu email address
  • Benefit from HSU's Humboldt Alumni Career Network (w/ thousands of alumni contacts)
    • Polish your interview skills through InterviewStream, an innovative online career tool (http://humboldt.interviewstream.com/)
    • Manage your job search through CareerBeam (your log-in info will be emailed to you this week)
    • Search out HSU alumni in positions and industries all across the country
  • Receive HSU's award winning Humboldt Magazine and the Boldt e-newsletter with news from your alma mater and updates on your fellow alumni
  • Register on the Humboldt Alumni website to search the Alumni Directory, submit a Class Note, create a Profile, join or create a Group, register for Events, renew your Membership, and more.

Plus there are Social Activities, Alumni Chapters, Library Privileges, Recreation Center Discount, Consumer Discounts, Humboldt State MasterCard, Group Insurance plans, Alumni Finder Service, Alumni Wine Club, Alumni Travel Program, and More!

If you have any questions just contact Humboldt Alumni at 826-3132 where Kim, Mykie or Devin will be happy to help you, or email us at alumni@humboldt.edu

For those who are graduating, please read the following regarding your Privacy Options:

The HSU Alumni Association has partnerships with companies that administer our affinity programs. These partnerships represent agreements between the University and the company to offer discounted services to alumni in areas such as travel, investment, insurance, or credit cards. In addition to discounts for alumni, these affinity partners give a portion of their business back to your Alumni Association in support of HSU, while also providing sponsorship opportunities for other events and programs.
