Dear Account Approver,

Our team at Marketing & Communications is excited to share the news that we are overhauling our chargeback system. This new system will be faster, more efficient, and give everyone more time to view and adjust the chartfields (account numbers) that will be charged. You are receiving this email because you have been identified as an Approver for your Program or Department for certain chartfields. As in the past, it will be up to you to identify any changes to your accounts prior to Marketing & Communications submitting chargebacks to Accounting every month. Here is how the new system will work starting 2/8/2023:

  1. You receive an email letting you know it is time to review jobs that have been completed with Marketing & Communications in the current chargeback period. You will only receive an email if you have charges to review.
  2. You will login to the system to review completed jobs based on the current chargeback period.
  3. If you have changes to any jobs, you will email marcom@humboldt.edu the changes with the associated job numbers. If you do not have changes, no action is needed.

We have created a guide to help navigate the new process.

Process Guide

FIRST TIME USERS: If you have not logged into our online ordering system yet, please go to humboldt.webdeskprint.com to login and register. You will use your Cal Poly Humboldt username and password to login. As a first time user, you will be prompted to register first. Once registered please email marcom@humboldt.edu to give you the correct permissions to view your chartfields.

If you have any questions about this process, or believe you have received this email in error, please contact Jimmy McCaffrey at x5144 or at jimmy@humboldt.edu.
