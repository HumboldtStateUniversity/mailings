Campus Community:

In July 2012, Payroll and ITS began phasing in PeopleSoft's Self-Service Absence Management System as part of a CSU system-wide standard leave accounting initiative. With the new system all faculty, staff and administrators will report absence information using this user-friendly on-line system rather than the paper form. Employees will log directly into PeopleSoft and enter either their absence information (sick time, vacation time, personal holiday, CTO time used), or "no leave taken" online. Validation and approval of employee absences will be completed by the appropriate administrators for each employee through the on-line PeopleSoft verification process.

Absence Management is a tool only to be used for attendance reporting; it does not change the departmental processes for requesting and approving time off. Additionally, employees with docks, Compensatory-time-off (CTO) earnings, Family Medical Leave, Overtime, Holiday Pay and Catastrophic Leave will continue the current practice of providing the applicable paper form to Payroll for processing.

Employees exempted from using the new system are: Intermittent Hourly Employees, Special Consultants, Extended Ed Faculty, Student Employees, and Auxiliary Organizations. They will continue to follow current manual practices.

Through the December 2012 pay period, absence reports will need to be submitted both on paper forms and electronically in PeopleSoft. This will allow the Payroll department to verify the system, send out the annual leave statement at the end of October 2012, and reconcile excess/deficit hours for 2012.
