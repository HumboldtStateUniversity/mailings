Campus Community,

We are providing the link below for detailed information on the annual Open Enrollment period for benefit plans to be effective January 1, 2011. The changes include:

  • Increases in health insurance premiums
  • Changes for HCRA reimbursable expenses
  • Age limit increase for eligible dependents from age 23 to under age 26 for continued health benefits

For comprehensive information regarding these changes and enrollment information, please access the following link:
http://www.humboldt.edu/hsuhr/employee/benefits/documents/OpenEnrollment2010.pdf
