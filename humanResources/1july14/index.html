The CSU is subject to federal equal employment opportunity and affirmative action regulations, which require us to reach out to, hire, and provide equal opportunity to qualified people with disabilities and qualified veterans. To help us examine our recruitment and hiring practices, we are requesting that you report on your current or past disability and veteran status.

Self-identification of disability and veteran status is voluntary, but we hope that you will choose to complete the process, whether or not you are a veteran or person with a disability. Neither your self-identification nor your refusal to self-identify will adversely affect your employment. Your self-identification is used for aggregate reporting purposes only.

To provide this self-identification and for definitions of disability and veteran categories, follow these steps:

  1. Login to myHumboldt (my.humboldt.edu)
  2. Return to this message and click the following link to report a current or past disability: Disability
  3. Return to this message and click the following link to report Veteran Status: Veteran Status

Please be sure that you are logged in to myHumboldt prior to clicking the above links; if you are not logged on, you will receive an error message.

A guide is available to assist you with the self-identification links if needed. If you have questions regarding this self-identification request, please contact Human Resources. Human Resources may be reached by phone at 707-826-3626 or by email at hsuhr@humboldt.edu.

Please note that if you have a disability and need accommodation, you may contact Human Resources to begin an interactive discussion to identify a reasonable accommodation.

We would appreciate your completion of this survey by October 1, 2014.

Sincerely,

Colleen Mullery
Senior Associate Vice President for Faculty Affairs and Human Resources
