ADDITIONAL RETIREMENT SERVICE CREDIT (ARSC; formerly known as AIR Time)

We have received notification from CALPERS that all requests for ARSC time must be "RECEIVED" BY CALPERS PRIOR TO January 1, 2013. Since Humboldt County does not have local access to a CalPERS regional office, requests for this time may be faxed to CalPERS no later than 5:00 p.m. on December 31, 2012, at 916-795-1224.

Campuses do not administer this program for CalPERS, if further information is needed please contact CalPERS directly at 888-225-7377.

Currently, eligible CalPERS members may purchase up to 5 years of additional retirement service credit. Effective January 1, 2013, this purchase option is no longer available to any members. In order to be eligible to purchase ARSC, a person must have five years of credited state service, and his or her application must be received by CalPERS prior to January 1, 2013. A person that does not have five years of service credit prior to January 1, 2013, will not be eligible to make the purchase, and therefore will be prohibited by law from purchasing ARSC.
