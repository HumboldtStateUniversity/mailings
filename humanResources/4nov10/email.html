Dear Colleagues:

The president has declared the Humboldt State University campus closed on the Friday following Thanksgiving, November 26, 2010. This day is not covered by a rescheduled holiday. Collective bargaining agreements allow for extension of holiday closure periods. As indicated on the Green and Gold Calendar, the campus will also be closed December 24, 2010 – January 2, 2011; this closure period is covered by observed and rescheduled holidays.

The University encourages employees to use their accrued vacation time or compensatory time on Friday, November 26, 2010 and take the day off. If you have insufficient leave credits (accrued vacation, CTO or personal holiday) to cover this day, contact your supervisor so arrangements can be made to provide sufficient work prior to the scheduled closure so you can take the time off. Such time worked shall be in accordance with Collective Bargaining Agreement language regarding overtime.

Employees may choose to work Friday, November 26, 2010. However, because the University will keep most campus buildings locked and closed during a closure for energy savings purposes, work assigned on this day may be in a different location or of a different nature than your normal work assignments. You must notify your supervisor of your intention to work this day by November 9, 2010. Supervisors will not be obliged to provide work or permission to work for employees who request to work after that date.

If you have any questions about the campus closure, use of leave credits, etc., please contact your supervisor, Human Resources or your union steward.

Tammy Curtis
Director, Human Resources
