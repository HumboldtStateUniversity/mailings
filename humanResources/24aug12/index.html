Campus Community:

The link below contains detailed information on the annual Open Enrollment period for benefit plans to be effective January 1, 2013.The changes and/or information include:

  • New HMO plan (Blue Shield Net Value) added to Humboldt County – no monthly employee premiums
  • Maximum annual contribution allowed for Health Care Reimbursement Account has decreased to $2,500. Dependent Care Reimbursement Account maximum annual contribution remains at $5,000.
  • Information for enrolling in Voluntary Plans.

For comprehensive information regarding these changes and other information, please access:

http://www.humboldt.edu/hsuhr/employee/benefits/documents/OELettertoCampus2012.pdf

We are also including a link to the Privacy Notice – Employee Assistance Program that the CSU is required to provide to employees:
