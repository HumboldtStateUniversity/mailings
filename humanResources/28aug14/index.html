TO: Benefit Eligible Employees

The annual Open Enrollment period will occur September 15 - October 10, with changes effective January 1, 2015. The following links will provide you important information regarding health benefits:

  • The health plan choices offered to HSU employees in 2015.
  • Comparison of health plans offered to HSU employees
  • The brochure for dental and voluntary benefits including information for the Health Care/Dependent Care Reimbursement accounts for 2015:
  • The premium rate sheet for 2015:
  • The enrollment form for Health Care Reimbursement Account (HCRA) and Dependent Care Reimbursement Account (DCRA)
  • Benefits Enrollment Worksheet to make plan changes, add, delete dependents, etc.

You may have already received notification from CalPERS indicating Blue Shield Net Value HMO will no longer be available in Humboldt County effective January 1, 2015. This letter advises employees to choose another health plan, and the impact if a change is not made during the Open Enrollment period.

Employees residing in Humboldt County who are currently enrolled in Blue Shield Net Value will need to choose a different health plan during the Annual Open Enrollment period (September 15 – October 10, 2014). If you are currently enrolled in Blue Shield Net Value and fail to choose a new health plan during open enrollment, CalPERS will automatically transfer you into PERS Choice Preferred Provider Organization (PPO) effective January 1, 2015.

Please complete the Benefits Enrollment Worksheet and choose your new plan. All open enrollment forms with original signatures must be received in the Human Resources office, Siemens Hall, Room 211, by 5:00 p.m., Friday, October 10, 2014. The available health plans for 2015 are:

  1. Blue Shield Access+ HMO
  2. Anthem Blue Cross Traditional HMO
  3. PERS Care PPO
  4. PERS Choice PPO
  5. PERS Select PPO
  6. PORAC PPO

*This plan is restricted to employees in Unit 8, State University Police Association (SUPA) and requires membership.

Please plan on attending the Benefits Fair on September 10, 2014, in Goodwin Forum, from 11:00 a.m. – 3:00 p.m., to learn more about the 2015 health benefit plan options. Health benefits plan representatives will be available at the Benefits Fair to address individual questions. Please access the FAQ which will address general questions regarding the loss of Blue Shield Net Value plan for 2015.

Employees may consider the Health Care Reimbursement Account (HCRA) as an option during open enrollment. This voluntary benefit plan offers employees the ability to pay for eligible out-of-pocket health care expenses with pre-tax dollars. Employees’ may contribute $20.00 to $208.33 each month ($2,500 maximum per year). Contributions are deducted from pay before federal, state and Social Security (FICA) taxes are withheld.

Please keep current with Open Enrollment information by visiting the Benefits website. You may also register for one of two Benefit Information sessions: Wednesday, September 3, 3:00 – 4:00, Goodwin Forum; and Thursday, September 4, 2:00 – 3:00, Goodwin Forum.
