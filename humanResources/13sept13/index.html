To Benefit Eligible Employees

Attached is information for Open Enrollment and new premium rates for HSU effective January 1, 2014. The Open Enrollment period is September 16 through October 11. All paperwork must be received in Human Resources by 5:00 p.m. on Friday October 11, 2013.

There is one new HMO offering in Humboldt County underwritten by Anthem Blue Cross. Please note that all HMO plans offer identical coverage including the same network doctors and hospitals, however, the premium will be different for each one. Please refer to the Premium Rate Sheet in the attachment.

HSU has scheduled a Benefits Fair for Wednesday, October 2, 1:00 – 4:00 p.m., in the Green & Gold Room of Founders Hall. Representatives for all of the health plans, some of the voluntary benefit plans and 403(b) plans will be in attendance to answer questions and provide information.

Thank you in advance for your commitment to delivering top-notch service to our students.

Sincerely,

David Bugbee
Associate Vice President of Human Resources
