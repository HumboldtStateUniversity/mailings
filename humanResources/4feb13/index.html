CSU Executive Order 1083 was issued and became effective on January 1, 2013 following the enactment of a recently amended state law called the California Child Abuse and Neglect Reporting Act. Pursuant to Executive Order 1083, every CSU employee is a mandated reporter under the California Child Abuse and Neglect Reporting Act and must sign a statement to that effect.

YOU ARE REQUIRED TO SUBMIT WRITTEN ACKNOWLEDGEMENT BY FEBRUARY 11, 2013 OF YOUR LEGAL RESPONSIBILITY TO REPORT ABUSE.

To comply with this requirement, please click here. Please print the document and complete the Acknowledgement of Responsibility including your name, department, signature and date. You may return the original document to Human Resources in Siemens Hall 211 or may send us a copy of your document by fax to 826-3625 or scan your signed document and email it to us at hsuhr@humboldt.edu. Please be sure to return your acknowledgement by February 11, 2013.

Highlights of Executive Order 1083 (Mandatory Reporting of Child Abuse and Neglect)

  • All CSU employees are designated mandated reporters of child abuse or neglect.
  • All HSU employees must submit acknowledgement to HSU Human Resources by February 11, 2013.
  • The Chancellor's office will provide additional information and training. HSU Human Resources will share this new information with you once it has been received.

The executive order is available for downloading at http://www.calstate.edu/eo/EO-1083.html. If you have any questions, please contact Nancy Resnick, the Mandated Reporting Coordinator, at 5174 or at ner13@humboldt.edu.

Thank you for your cooperation.
