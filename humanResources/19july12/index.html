Absence Management/New Leave Accounting System

Good news! HSU is migrating to the PeopleSoft Core Absence Management System. This is the system that will eventually replace the current leave tracking system, CLAS. In keeping with HSU's priorities regarding sustainability and being a green campus, Payroll will be transitioning to self-service absence management which involves electronic submission of leave usages, etc. There will be some situations that will require paper absence reports but the majority will be by electronic submission to Payroll. This change will only involve the reporting of leave usage to Payroll and will eliminate the need for the paper process currently used. Departmental needs may still require paper submission. Employees will also be able to view their individual leave balances in real time through PeopleSoft. The following departments have graciously agreed to be pilots for this transition to self-service beginning with the July pay period:

  • Plant Operations
  • ITS
  • Housing & Dining
  • Human Resources
  • University Police Department

With the success of transition for the pilot departments, other departments will transition over the next several months. Training for employees, attendance clerks and managers will be rolled out in the near future. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact Patty Ambrosini at 826-4915.

Other New Features for Employees

With the availability of self-service, employees can view their individual compensation history and view/change their individual emergency contact information. More information regarding these new features will be forthcoming.
