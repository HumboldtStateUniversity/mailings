Furlough Date Change

The campus has found it necessary to make a change to the Staff and Management Furlough Calendar. The change is necessary because of Humboldt Preview, which is typically offered when high school students have their Easter/Spring break. This year the logical date for Humboldt Preview is April 9, 2009, which had been identified as a staff and management furlough day. After consultation, the furlough date has been changed to Friday, April 16, 2010. We realize this change may have an impact on staff/managers and their families. We are asking that administrators be flexible in the rescheduling of this furlough day and work with employees to mitigate any impact. The on-line Staff and Management Furlough Calendar and furlough plan information have been updated to reflect this change.

Health Fair Reminder

Friday, September 11, 2009 at 12:00 - 2:00pm, Goodwin Forum

Representatives from the following companies will be available to answer questions:

  • Anthem Blue Cross (underwriter of PERS Choice, PERS Select and PERS Care PPO plans)
  • Blue Shield HMO
  • Humboldt-Del Norte Foundation for Medical Care (local liaison with Blue Shield HMO)
  • Sanders Voluntary Insurance Company
  • Cal-Ore Life Flight Program

This is an excellent opportunity for employees to compare specific details of these plans for making an informed choice for their health insurance needs for 2010. Sanders Insurance Company will be available to answer questions regarding the voluntary insurance plans—disability income, accidental death & dismemberment, hospital income and cancer/other catastrophic diseases medical reimbursement plan.

This year Cal-Ore Life Flight Program will be attending to provide information to HSU employees on enrollment in their air ambulance program at a discounted rate. This program transports patients who need medical services that cannot be provided locally, via air ambulance, to higher medical care—primarily to hospitals in San Francisco and Redding.

Reservations are not required; all benefit-eligible employees and spouses/partners are encouraged to attend. For additional information please call extension 3626.
