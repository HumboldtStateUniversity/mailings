Colleagues:

Because additional informal time off was granted by the university on December 24, 2012, some employees were unable to reduce their vacation accrual credits down to the maximum allowable carryover by the end of the year.

If you are in this situation, you will be granted excess vacation carryover of up to one full workday for the 2013 calendar year. Payroll will determine who this applies to and the additional hours will be reflected in PeopleSoft after February 1.

You can view your leave accrual balances by accessing PeopleSoft and navigating to Self Service/Time Reporting/Employee Balance Inquiry. If you have any questions please contact Shanna Del Grande in Payroll at 826-4910.
