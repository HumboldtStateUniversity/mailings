Campus Community,

Below is information on new voluntary benefit offerings for the CSU.

Please note that HSU has scheduled:

Voluntary Benefits Fair
Friday, March 11, 10:00 - 2:00
Green & Gold Room, Founders Hall

Vendors from the four voluntary benefits plans will be available to provide information and enrollment assistance to employees. Enrollment in these plans must occur during the special open enrollment period—March 1 - March 31, 2011. NOTE: For all plans, employees must enroll directly with the appropriate company through their website, by calling their toll-free telephone number or speaking directly with a company representative at the Voluntary Benefits Fair.



California State University (CSU) is pleased to offer several new Group Voluntary Benefits that were specifically designed to protect your income and assets. These products include:

  • MetLaw® Group Legal Plan - Hyatt Legal Plans, a MetLife® Company
  • Group Critical Illness Insurance - Aflac
  • Accidental Death and Dismemberment (AD&D) Insurance - The Standard
  • Voluntary Long-Term Disability (LTD) Insurance - The Standard
  • Auto and Home Insurance - California Casualty

Each offering listed above serves a unique purpose, and your individual needs will affect the types of insurance you will want to purchase to protect you and your loved ones from unpredictable life situations.

Some of the plans require that you be benefits eligible; some do not.

A special open enrollment will be held March 1 - March 31, 2011, and benefits will become effective May 1, 2011.

An added feature of the open enrollment: The Standard is offering currently enrolled employees, and employees who previously waived this benefit, a final opportunity to enroll in Voluntary Life Insurance (if not previously declined by The Standard). This special opportunity allows employees to increase coverage or enroll up to $100,000—without having to answer medical questions.
