Dear Humboldt Parent,

I'm writing to request your assistance with a new website that we are creating for parents and families with students at Humboldt State University.

The site is designed to help you and other Humboldt families talk to and learn from each other. You can post a question, start a discussion, and share your experiences with others. It will feature short profiles of each participant, and you can share as much or as little information about yourself as you'd like.

Before the site gets up and running, we'd like to have a few profiles ready to go so we're asking those who already subscribe to the Humboldt parent listserv to be the first to participate.

To get started, just visit HSUfamilynetwork.org and register for the site. All you need to do is answer a few questions, attach a picture (if desired), and then start posting.

Thanks for your assistance. We hope that this website helps Humboldt parents and families learn from the true experts—each other.

Sincerely,

Randi Darnall Burke
Dean of Students
