Colleagues,

As chair of the campus nominating committee for the awarding of honorary degrees, I invite your nominations for honorary degrees to be conferred at the May 12, 2012 Commencement Ceremonies. Nominations are due to the committee by Monday, November 7, 2011.

The CSU guidelines are contained within a 2010 memo at http://www.calstate.edu/AcadAff/codedmemos/AA-2010-24.pdf and the HSU Executive Memorandum guiding the nomination process is at http://www.humboldt.edu/president/P0604.html. Please note that the process is confidential, and campus representatives may not contact prospective nominees to discuss possible nominations.

The timeline for the process this year is as follows:

November 7 – Nominations for honorary degrees are due to the campus committee. Please email nominations and supporting documents to Mary Kay Hartman, Commencement Coordinator at mkh32@humboldt.edu.

Early December – Recommendations from President Richmond are due to the CSU Board of Trustees.

February 2012 – President Richmond informs recipient(s) of the award and invites them to participate in the 2012 Commencement Ceremony.

May 12, 2012 – Honorary degree(s) awarded at Commencement Ceremonies.

Thank you and please let me know if you have any questions.

Frank Whitlatch
Interim Vice President for University Advancement
