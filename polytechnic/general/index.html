A Message from Humboldt State University

January 24, 2022

Reminder: Final Step in Polytechnic Designation Process and Watch Party

Dear [First Name]:
I have some exciting news: Humboldt State University is now officially California State Polytechnic University, Humboldt! But you can call us Cal Poly Humboldt.
We are the first polytechnic in Northern California and only the third in the state. It's a historic moment for the Humboldt community, and it’s an incredible opportunity for you as well.
A polytechnic embraces a "learn by doing" approach to education. Academic programming is broad in natural sciences, applied sciences, technology, and engineering. It has a strong liberal arts curriculum. It emphasizes hands-on learning, helping students apply what they learn to prepare graduates for that next step in life, whether that’s a career or graduate school.
In other words, it's everything we already are; however, with a formal designation, we have the state funding and resources to provide you a bigger and better educational experience over the next several years. To name a few, we are:
  • Launching 12 new academic programs by Fall 2023
  • Building a new science building, upgrading lab and classroom spaces
  • Building additional on- and off-campus housing
  • Upgrading broadband and other technology
Here’s what you need to know right now:
  • Academics: We are not cutting programs—only adding them!
  • Admissions: While you were admitted to "Humboldt State University," you're now officially accepted to “Cal Poly Humboldt.” Congrats!
  • Tuition: Becoming a polytechnic will not increase tuition, which is determined by the California State University.
At our core, we're still the Humboldt that thousands of current students and alumni know and love. We’re a close-knit community of faculty who care about your academic and personal growth. They often work with you one-on-one and many become mentors, too. Staff are also ready to support you through cultural resources, tutoring, or other services.
What makes Humboldt special is the region’s unparalleled beauty. Students from across California live and learn in a stunning location where ancient redwoods and the Pacific Ocean are just minutes away.
No doubt you have more questions so please feel free to reach out to me and check out the FAQ on the polytechnic website for details. It’s an amazing time and I can’t wait for you to join us this Fall.
Confirm your enrollment to Cal Poly Humboldt today!
Peter Martinez
Director of Admissions
