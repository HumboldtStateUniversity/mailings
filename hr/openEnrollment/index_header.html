OPEN ENROLLMENT – BENEFITS; EFFECTIVE JANUARY 1, 2016
BENEFITS FAIR – THURSDAY, SEPTEMBER 3, GOODWIN FORUM; 10am – 2pm

The CSU annual Open Enrollment period will occur September 14, 2015 through October 9, 2015. Open Enrollment is your annual opportunity to review and update your current benefits for the upcoming year. You can enroll, change or cancel CalPERS health, dental, vision, Flexcash, Dependent Care Reimbursement (DCRA), Health Care Reimbursement (HCRA) or voluntary benefits plans. The effective date for all changes/enrollments made during the Open Enrollment period will be January 1, 2016.

NOTE: For 2016, the premiums for Anthem Blue Cross Traditional HMO will cost less than the premiums for Blue Shield Access+ HMO. The level of coverage, benefits and provider network in Humboldt County are the same, however prescription coverage may vary. If you have questions regarding the coverage of specific medications in a health plan, please refer to the Open Enrollment Benefits Communication (link below) for prescription plan contact information. Both HMO plans are facilitated through the Independent Physicians Association (IPA) in Eureka; the PPO plans are administered directly by Anthem Blue Cross.

The following links will provide you with detailed information for Open Enrollment and the necessary forms to make changes and/or enroll.

  • The Open Enrollment Benefits Communication – for the 2016 plan year.
  • To make a health plan change, or add or delete dependents for health and dental insurance, please access the Benefits Enrollment Worksheet for Open Enrollment.
  • To enroll or re-enroll for the Health Care Reimbursement (HCRA) and Dependent Care Reimbursement Account (DCRA). This voluntary benefit plan offers employees the ability to pay for eligible out-of-pocket health care expenses and/or dependent care expenses with pre-tax dollars. Additional Information is in the Open Enrollment Benefits Communication (above).
  • The health benefits premium rate sheet for 2016.
  • All open enrollment forms with original signatures must be received in the Human Resources office, Siemens Hall, Room 212, by 5:00 p.m., Friday, October 9, 2015.
  • All information regarding Open Enrollment will be available on the Benefits Website.

Please plan on attending the annual Benefits Fair scheduled for Thursday, September 3, 10:00 – 2:00, in Goodwin Forum.

Thank you.
