A Message from Humboldt State University

September 19, 2019

2019 Clery Act Crime Report

Dear HSU Community,

Pursuant to the Jeanne Clery Disclosure of Campus Security Policy and Campus Crime Statistics Act, the 2019 Annual Security Report (ASR) for Humboldt State is now available for viewing.

The ASR contains the current security and safety-related policy statements, emergency preparedness and evacuation information, crime prevention and sexual assault prevention information, and drug and alcohol prevention programming. The ASR also contains statistics of Clery Act crimes for Humboldt State University for 2016 - 2018.

A paper copy of the ASR is available upon request by contacting the Office of the Clery Director located at 1 Harpst Street, Arcata (Student Business Services Rm 345).
