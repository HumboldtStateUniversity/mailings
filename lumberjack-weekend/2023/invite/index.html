Get ready to show your Green and Gold spirit for Humboldt's Lumberjack Weekend, formerly known as Homecoming & Family Weekend..
Thursday, Oct. 19–22
This weekend is all about building pride and celebrating our Lumberjack family of students, staff, faculty, alumni, families, and friends.
While students are not required to register, we kindly request alumni, family, and friends to register.
Event Highlights
Lumberjack Weekend activities begin Thursday evening with the Kick-Off Party at 6 p.m. at the University Quad followed by the Lumber-Snatched Drag Show, 8 - 10 p.m., in the Gutswurrak Student Activities Center.
Other highlights include the Fall Maker’s Market on Friday, 11 a.m. - 3 p.m., at the University Quad, featuring student and staff craft vendors, and a Forever Humboldt Arcata Marsh Walk on Sunday morning at 10 a.m.
Main Event
Don’t miss the Lumberjack Weekend Block Party Saturday at 1:30 - 8 p.m. in the G11 and FS9 parking lots.
Live music by Remi Wolf, Sage The Gemini, The Altons, The California Poppies, and the Soul Party Crew. There will be activities for the whole family, including face painting, live screen printing, axe throwing, food trucks, and more.

Parking
As part of the Lumberjack Weekend Block Party, some parking lots will be closed on Friday, Oct. 20 and Saturday, Oct. 21:
  • Lot FS9—Closed all day Friday, Oct. 20 to 1 p.m. on Sunday, Oct. 22
  • Lot G11—The east part of the lot will be closed from 5 p.m. Friday, Oct. 20, to 1 p.m. on Sunday, Oct. 22. While you may still park in the west section of the lot, if you move your car during the event, you won’t be able to return until Saturday after 8 p.m.
Consider alternative modes of transportation or park in a different lot on Friday, Oct. 20, and Saturday, Oct. 21.
