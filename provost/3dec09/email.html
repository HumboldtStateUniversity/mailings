Dear Colleagues,

The Diversity Plan Action Council (DPAC) and the Center for Excellence in Learning and Teaching (CELT) will combine efforts to offer The Institute for Diversity in Learning & Teaching. It will be May 19 and 20, 2010.

In order to meet the needs of our students, the Institute will offer faculty, staff and administrators information, resources, and strategies useful in supporting and understanding student learning. Workshops, invited speakers, and keynote presenters will focus on offering various strategies successful in meeting the needs of diverse learners.

Room locations and workshop times are being planned.

Please mark your calendars and plan to participate in this event.
