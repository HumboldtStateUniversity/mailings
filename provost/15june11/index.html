Dear Faculty and Staff Colleagues,

Humboldt State University must comply with The California State University Board of Trustees direction that no one may modify, remodel or repair buildings on state property or space leased by the state without approval of the HSU Deputy Building Official ( Mr. Mark Baker, Facilities Management, 826-3646).

• Faculty and staff are not authorized to make any alterations, remodeling, modifications, repairs or perform maintenance on any campus physical structures including buildings, offices, classroom, library, laboratories, computer labs, playing fields or grounds.

• Requests for maintenance, remodeling or repairs are made to Facilities Management (Plant Operations) at 826-3646.

• Simple modifications costing under $200 are made by Plant Operations without charge per the University Management Letter 00-05 (Plant Operations Chargeback Policy)

Life safety hazards and liabilities have been incurred by the University when modification/repairs, etc. are not completed by authorized personnel. Under no circumstances should unauthorized staff or faculty make alterations to permanent structures on campus. Such alterations must be approved by and made by personnel in Plant Operations.
