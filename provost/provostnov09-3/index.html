Campus community,

Representatives from San Diego State University and CSU Long Beach will be visiting HSU to discuss their University-wide governance structure on Nov. 5 and 13, respectively.

All university faculty, staff, administrators and students are invited to attend an Open Forum for questions and discussion with the SDSU and CSULB representatives. The purpose of their visit is to allow us to ask them about how governance works on their campuses, and whether their governance models, or components of their models, may be desirable for our campus to adopt.

SDSU Open Forum: Thursday, Nov. 5, 10 to 11:30 a.m., University Banquet Room

CSU LB Open Forum: Friday, Nov. 13, 10 to 11:30 a.m., University Banquet Room

Each member of these panels is actively involved in campus governance. This is an excellent opportunity to explore alternate models of governance, and we hope that you can join us at both of these open forums. Please note that the University Banquet Room is located behind the Depot (north entrance).
