Dear HSU Faculty & Staff,

As we prepare to close the academic year, I would like to encourage you to participate in HSU's Institute for Student Success. This two-day event will take place on campus May 18th and 19th, and will include a variety of workshops and keynote presentations focused on providing faculty, staff and administrators with information, resources and strategies to support a variety of student learning needs.

A complete agenda and online registration for the Institute are available at humboldt.edu/institute (Note that registration closes this Friday, May 13th.)

As you are all aware, improving the success and supporting the learning needs of students from all backgrounds is one of the university's core commitments. These goals are central to the Graduation Rate Improvement initiative that is currently being implemented across the CSU system. While achieving those goals will surely require a collective university-wide effort that exceeds any individual role, it is my firm belief that each one of us on campus must participate in that process if we are truly to meet that goal. Whether you are a staff or a faculty member, this Institute is designed to offer you the tools you need to fully participate in that process.

I strongly encourage you to take advantage of this unique opportunity.

Sincerely,

Robert Snyder
Provost and VP
