Please join us for an important campus forum with the newly formed Cabinet for Institutional Change from noon to 1 p.m., Monday, March 9 in the Kate Buchanan Room (KBR). Bring your lunch, and we'll bring dessert.

At the forum, you'll have a chance to meet the members of the Cabinet, find out about the major focus areas of change, and learn how you can share your ideas and get involved. The group has been asked to guide a complicated change process involving all aspects of the University, and we need broad participation from the campus community to be successful.

Our focus is on five areas: realizing our University vision; improving campus governance; achieving student success; creating a culture of evidence; and cultivating a collegial, respectful and responsive community.

We hope to see you on Monday.
