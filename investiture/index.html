Dear Friends,

Though power has been restored to campus and to most of Humboldt County as of early this morning, campus remains closed. Classes and regular campus operations are anticipated to resume tomorrow, Friday, Oct. 11.

Campus updates on the outage
 

For those who were planning to attend the Presidential Investiture events, we are working to reschedule all the activities that were originally planned, including the Investiture Ceremony, Reception, and Scholarship Dinner.

If you’re unable to attend on the rescheduled date, your event donation can be refunded or you can donate the $150 to the scholarship fund.

Email invites will be sent with details once they have been finalized.

 

Sincerely,

Humboldt State University
