A Message from Humboldt State University

April 23, 2018

Loss of Student Malachi Ennis

Dear University Community:

We are sad to share news of the loss of Humboldt State University student Malachi Ennis.

Malachi, of Trinidad, California died Sunday, April 22. He was a Music major who had transferred to HSU from College of the Redwoods in fall 2016. He is remembered as a kind, diligent student who took responsibility for learning coursework material.

We extend our condolences to his family and friends. His death is a loss for our community, and our thoughts are with all of those who were close to him.

University staff have reached out to provide support to students, faculty, and staff who may have known him.

Any students in need of additional support during this time are urged to consider contacting the Dean of Students office at 707.826.3504 or in Siemens Hall 211, or Counseling and Psychological Services at 707.826.3236 or on the second floor of the Student Health Center. Staff or faculty seeking additional support may utilize the Employee Assistance Program at 707.443.7358.
