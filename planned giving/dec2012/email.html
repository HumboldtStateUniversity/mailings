If you are age 75 or greater, Humboldt State University's Charitable Gift Annuity program can now offer you a higher income for your retirement needs.

HSU's famous Marching Lumberjacks

What is a Gift Annuity? It’s a simple contract between you and HSU’s gift annuity program. In return for a contribution, we pay you a secure, fixed life-time income. When the annuity ends, the remaining funds benefit HSU students, faculty, and programs.

New rates range from 6.5% for a 75-year-old to 9.8% for those ages 90+.*

Other benefits include:

  • Fixed, lifetime income that is partially tax-free
  • Charitable income tax deduction, reducing your taxes this year
  • Good feelings — knowing you are making a difference in the lives of students at HSU
  • It’s simple to start a gift annuity — we can even do it all by mail

* Rates remain attractive for individuals 55-74, and for couples.

Contact us to learn more, and to request a personalized illustration showing how a Charitable Gift Annuity can work for you.
