Colleagues,

Last year, as part of a Humboldt County initiative to bring Delta Airlines into the county, Humboldt State University made a commitment to Delta Airlines to spend $75,000 for flight travel by June 2009. To date, the campus has spent about $18,000 toward that commitment, and the campus needs to fulfill its commitment by fully spending the $75,000.

Therefore, effective February 25, 2009, on a first-come, first-served basis, the University will pay 100 percent of the flight cost booked on Delta Airlines until the University's commitment is fully realized. This travel opportunity is for Humboldt State University employees on University business only. Travel must be completed by the end of May. Follow your regular travel authorization process. You must have your travel plans approved by an authorized campus administrator prior to purchasing your Delta ticket. Book your travel using the regular procedures for Giselle's Travel, but request to fly on Delta Airlines. Send your paperwork through the regular way, and our Accounts Payable staff will pay the cost of your airline ticket. If you have any questions, please call Linda Herman at 826-5791.

Check out the Delta flight options for flying south and see if they work for you. Just checking similar flight schedules for Arcata (ACV) to Los Angeles (LAX), note that even traveling through Delta's hub in Salt Lake City, one arrives in Los Angeles very close to the United Airlines* flight times, which connect through San Francisco. Your advantage may be that you will not be controlled or grounded by San Francisco weather.
