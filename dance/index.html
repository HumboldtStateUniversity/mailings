DON'T MISS THE DANCE OF THE CENTURY!

Dear Students,

This year has been full of excitement, activities and special events to celebrate Humboldt State University's 100th year. This Friday, April 25 we will cap off the Centennial celebration with The Dance of the Century in Lumberjack Arena. The whole HSU community is invited!

Friday, April 25, in memory of those lost in the April 10 bus accident, the HSU Advancement Foundation will match donations made to the two memorial scholarships that have been established. And at the dance, the Foundation will give $5 to the scholarships for each of the first 1,000 people who attend to show their support. The Foundation has pledged up to $20,000 for these efforts.

DETAILS
Friday, April 25, 2014
8pm–Midnight
Lumberjack Arena in the Kinesiology & Athletics Building

COST
FREE—includes entry, food and more. No host bar (21+).
SPECIAL INCENTIVE—Pre-register today and receive extra raffle tickets at the door.

WHAT'S HAPPENING?

Dress in your favorite decade and dance the night away. There will be amazing free raffle prizes, awesome music, free food, no-host speakeasy lounge with beer and wine, karaoke, dance performances, special screenings of the Centennial Documentary, a photo booth, games and more.

Check out the full list of raffle prizes and more at humboldt.edu/dance
To learn more about the April 25 Founders Day on campus visit humboldt.edu/foundersday

Grab a costume, mark your calendar and join us for an amazing Centennial Finale with your Humboldt Family!
