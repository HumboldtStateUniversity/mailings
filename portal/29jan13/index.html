January 29, 2013

myHumboldt now links directly to CSYou

Effective February 1, 2013, you'll see a new CSYou icon on myHumboldt in place of the CSU Portal logo. Clicking this icon will take you directly to CSYou, the new, more personalized CSU portal.

What does this mean for HSU?

In practical terms, the main changes for us are different routes to reach certain central CSU applications and services:

  • The ServiceNow CSU helpdesk ticketing system for PeopleSoft maintenance and development issues will be accessible through CSYou -> Tools & Services -> Computers & Technology->Contact IT Support
  • PeopleSoft CFS will be accessible through CSYou -> Tools & Services -> Financial Tools -> CFS - Financial Login, as well as through the direct PeopleSoft Finance link at myHumboldt.
  • Financial Reporting will be accessible through CSYou -> Tools & Services -> Financial Tools -> Data Warehouse. The CSU Portal -> Financial Reports -> Reports login -> Login link will still work, so if you've bookmarked this link, you won't need to change how you access Financial Reporting.

CSYou now serves as the access point for all information for employees only that was formerly located at www.calstate.edu, such as Systemwide HR Benefits or Systemwide Accounting.

From this central site, employees can access a range of tools and services and find campus as well as systemwide news, web-based services, documents, and data specifically intended for employees. It includes customizable menus, restructured navigation, a searchable systemwide employee directory, and media-rich content such as videos and podcasts.

If you have any questions about how CSYou works and how you can make best use of it, please refer to the Tutorial and FAQs on the CSYou site at http://csyou.calstate.edu/csyouhelp.
