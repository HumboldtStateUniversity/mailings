Dear Staff and Faculty,

I am pleased to announce that Mike Burghart has accepted the appointment of Interim Director of Contracts, Procurement, and Risk Management. He will replace Dave Bugbee who was named the new AVP for Human Resources last week.

Mike has over six years as an accountant in HSU's Financial Services and during that time he has progressively taken on additional responsibilities and successfully managed the accounting services for the Sponsored Programs Foundation and Advancement Foundation. In addition, Mike has been a key member of the implementation team that has transitioned the Campus, Sponsored Programs Foundation, and Advancement Foundation to the Common Financial administrative software system, including the financial module, asset management, student financial services module, and the procurement /contracts module. Prior to this, Mike worked in a similar capacity for the Pacific Lumber Company, Simpson Paper Company, and Coast Central Credit Union. Mike is a graduate of HSU with a degree in Business Administration with concentrations in Accounting and Finance.

Burt Nordstrom,
Vice President for Administrative Affairs
