To ease campus access for finals week, end-of-semester departures and Commencement exercises, Humboldt State University will temporarily reopen Harpst Street for eastbound, one-way traffic between L.K. Wood Boulevard and Rossow Street from Monday, May 11, through Sunday, May 17.

Harpst Street between Rossow and B Streets will remain two-way. Redwood Manor entrance into the Mai Kai lot will be closed effective 5pm Friday, May 8th to allow manor residents to move out for the summer. Exiting the campus will continue via B Street to 14th Street.

Harpst Street will close again effective Monday, May 18. Alternate access will be through Redwood Manor Apartments Drive from L.K. Wood, or from 14th Street. Harpst Street will not reopen until the start of fall semester, where it will again be eastbound, one-way traffic.

Burt Nordstrom
Vice President of Administrative Affairs
