Dear Campus Community,

On the morning of Saturday, Dec. 22 the campus phone system will be down for maintenance. The phone outage is expected to last from 5 to 7 a.m.

During the outage, the University Police Department phone lines will not function. If you have an emergency, you should call 911.

University Police has a cell phone number that may be reached during the outage at
(707) 273-2700. This number is not published, and is only used when the regular phone system is not available. It is a single line, and callers will need to call back if it is busy.

This phone outage is necessary to fix a congestion problem, and was scheduled to minimize disruptions.

Sincerely,

Joyce Lopes
Vice President of Administrative Affairs
