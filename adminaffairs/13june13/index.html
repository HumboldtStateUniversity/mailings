Dear HSU Students, Faculty and Staff:

Humboldt State University is proud to host the graduation ceremonies for the Northern Humboldt Union High School District. The Arcata High and McKinleyville High School graduations will take place at Humboldt State University's Redwood Bowl.

The scheduled events are as follows:

Thursday, June 13, 2013
McKinleyville High School Graduation - 2:30 PM to 4:30 PM.
Arcata High Graduation - 5:30 PM to 7:30 PM.

During the graduation ceremonies held on Thursday, parking may be limited. In addition, access to areas north of Union and 17th Streets will be restricted. Please plan accordingly.

We appreciate your help in directing students, parents, friends and family members during this event.

Thank you,
