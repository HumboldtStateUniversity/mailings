Dear Colleagues,

Please join me in welcoming David Bugbee as the new Associate Vice President for Human Resources. David has over twenty years of progressive human resources experience in both the private and public sector. This experience has provided David with a unique skill set and knowledge of human resource issues.

I want to thank the search committee for all the many hours that they worked on this successful search, particularly Randi Darnall Burke for her leadership in chairing the committee. Also I want to thank Teresa Stanley, Nancy Hurlbut, Anna Kircher, Jim Stemach, Kumi Watanabe-Schock, Colleen Mullery, Michael Burghart, and Patty Ambrosini for their important work on the committee.

Over the next month, Tammy Curtis will be working with Dave to assure a smooth transition. I want to issue a very special thank you to Tammy Curtis, who has served HSU for over 35 years with style and class. On behalf of all of us that have worked with Tammy, we wish her and her husband Dan all the best in retirement.

Burt Nordstrom
Vice President for Administrative Affairs
