Seasons Greetings,

As you make plans to travel during the semester break, please be sure that you are prepared for winter weather. Expect to encounter heavy rain, hail, ice and/or snow on the highways. It is advisable to carry traction devices, such as chains or cables for your car, and, most important, slow down. We want you to arrive safely. We at Humboldt care about your safety.

It is a good idea to consult the National Weather Service at 707-443-7062, listen to NOAA weather radio or online at http://www.weather.gov/.

Road conditions are available by phoning 800-427-7623 or online at http://www.dot.ca.gov/cgi-bin/roads.cgi. Local highways are 101, 299, 199, and 36.
