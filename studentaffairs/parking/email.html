HSU Faculty and Staff,

Our annual Homecoming & Family Weekend and Fall Preview events will be happening this weekend (Friday, Saturday and Sunday, Oct. 15 – 17) and will bring more than 1000 alumni, prospective students and their families to our campus. Also joining us on campus are prospective students and parents for our TRiO/EOP programs, as well as participants in Indigenous Peoples Week activities.

This is our chance to welcome many alumni, students and prospective students, some who are returning to campus for the first time since graduation and some who are new to the Humboldt State experience.

Hosting so many guests means that parking spaces will be scarce and one way to save room is by finding alternative transportation to campus. We suggest riding the bus with the JackPass, which you can do for free on Friday, Oct. 15.
