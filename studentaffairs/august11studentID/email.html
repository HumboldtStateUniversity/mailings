Dear HSU Students,

Registration validation stickers are no longer needed on the student ID card. You will not have to stand in line at the cashier window to obtain your sticker. The magnetic strip on your ID card will be used to verify your current student status and to provide access to campus and bus services.

Simply present your ID card to be swiped to receive services at the Cashier's office, Financial Aid, Registrar's office, pool, gym, athletic events, library, media distribution, Health and Counseling Center, student labs and for the Jack Pass.

Life should be a bit simpler now.

Edward M. Webb
Vice President, Student Affairs
