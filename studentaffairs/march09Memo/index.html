Dear Faculty,

Each spring Humboldt State University's Outstanding Student Awards Ceremony recognizes many students for their outstanding accomplishments in and out of the classroom. This year's ceremony is scheduled for Thursday, April 30, from 3:00 — 5:00 p.m. in the Kate Buchanan Room. Please mark your calendar now and plan to join us.

This is a call to faculty to nominate outstanding students for each of Humboldt's annual awards as listed below. This year we have added new academic categories which highlight Academic Excellence and Achievement. The award categories are:

  • Award for Excellence in an Academic Discipline (one award per college)
  • Awards for Academic Excellence and Achievement
    • Best Undergraduate Research Project
    • Best Individual Artistic Achievement in Visual and Performing Arts
    • Best Poster Presentation to a Professional Association
    • Best Professional Publication
    • Best Forensic/Debate Presentation or Competition (Individual or Team)
    • Patricia O. McConkey Award for Outstanding Graduate Thesis/Project (coordinated by Research & Graduate Studies)
  • Outstanding Contribution to a Campus Club, Program, or Organization
  • Outstanding Contribution to an Associated Students Program
  • Al Elpusan Award for Student Activism
  • Award for Excellence in Community Service
  • Award for Excellence in Intercollegiate Athletics or Sport Clubs
  • Brian Lorensen Residence Life Staff Award

Additionally, two Outstanding Students of the Year will be selected from amongst the top candidates in the above categories.

We encourage you to nominate your outstanding students!

Nomination descriptions, criteria and forms are available online at the Student Affairs website http://studentaffairs.humboldt.edu/studentawards.

I hope you will be able to join us at this year's event.

Sincerely,

Steven Butler
Vice President of Student Affairs
