Lumberjacks!

The 2010 California election is approaching and students from all over the state are getting involved. Be part of the movement that's getting young people registered to vote and out to the polls!

Campuses from San Diego to Humboldt are taking part in a bold initiative to register 30,000 students throughout the California State University system. The November 2nd election will be a critical decision-making time for California voters that will affect the direction of our state, our economy, and higher education. Here at Humboldt, Associated Students has a goal of registering 800 HSU students and we need your help. HSU is competing against CSU Stanislaus to register more students, and this is your call to action. Show your Lumberjack pride and represent Humboldt State! Register to VOTE!

You must register to vote by October 18, 2010.

Election Day is November 2, 2010.

Voter registration will be taking place in the quad, or you can stop by the Associated Students office in the University Center building. Contact Associated Students at 707-826-4221 or visit www.csustudents.org for more information.

Go Lumberjacks!
