HSU Faculty and Staff,

Our annual Spring Preview event is this Friday and Saturday (April 9 & 10) and will bring more than 1000 prospective students and their families to our campus. This is our chance to welcome many students who will be part of the incoming class for Fall 2010. The welcoming program begins at 4:00 PM on Friday in the Kinesiology & Athletics Building (K.A.), and you are invited to come and meet with your prospective students. Hosting so many potential students means that parking spaces will be scarce; if you can find alternative transportation on Friday to free-up spaces for our visitors, it would be appreciated. See you at the K.A. reception.
