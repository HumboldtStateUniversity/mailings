HSU Faculty and Staff:

This week we will welcome more than 800 prospective students and their families to campus for our annual Spring Preview event, held this Friday, April 12th. One of the largest activities taking place during Spring Preview is the Preview Reception, which will be held on Friday from 3:00 - 4:30 PM in the West Gymnasium, 2nd Floor, Recreation & Wellness Center. You are invited and encouraged to attend this reception and meet with our prospective students and their families.

We also wish to remind you that due to the high Spring Preview attendance, on-campus parking may be scarce on Friday, April 12th; we encourage you to find alternative transportation to campus that day if possible.

To see the full schedule of events for Spring Preview, please visit http://www.humboldt.edu/springpreview/

Thank you for supporting this event and welcoming our prospective students and families! We hope to see you at the reception Friday afternoon.

Sincerely,
