Dear HSU students,

WELCOME! We are off to an exciting year with over 1300 new freshmen and almost 900 transfer students coming to campus. Included are students from many other states and countries. Along with returning students, our total enrollment will approach 8000.

There are many activities planned for the first several weeks that are designed to acquaint and connect you with campus services and programs. For example, during the first week of school there are three concerts in the Depot sponsored by the Associated Students and a music jam on the Cypress Lawn. During the second week there is a barbeque and a Volunteer Fair; followed by another concert, the Clubs Fair, as well as soccer and football games.

If you are interested in campus governance, the Associated Students is the place to go. There are openings on many campus committees and on the A.S. Council.

Check it out at http://www.humboldt.edu/welcomehome

The key to your success at Humboldt is "involvement." It works.

Welcome home!

Edward M. Webb
Vice President, Student Affairs
