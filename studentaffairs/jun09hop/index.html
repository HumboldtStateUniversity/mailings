Dear Faculty and Staff,

We are once again expecting over a thousand prospective freshmen to attend the Humboldt Orientation Program (HOP). HOP has programs to "seal-the-deal" with new students, e.g., academic advising, peer and college connections, learning-success strategies and course registration. Additionally, we offer orientation programs for the parents of the student participants.

As part of the HOP Parent Program we will be holding welcome receptions for the families/guests of our new students so that they may meet our academic leaders, faculty and staff. We are extending an invitation to you to join us. This is an opportunity for the parents and guests of our new students to get acquainted and to ask questions about HSU and your programs. Your presence would add to the personal touch HSU offers. These receptions will be held at the Aquatics Center in Eureka. Beverages and hors d'oeuvres will be provided at the receptions as well as bus service to and from HSU to the venue. The Humboldt Bay Aquatic Center is located at 921 Waterfront Drive next to the Adorni Center in Eureka.
