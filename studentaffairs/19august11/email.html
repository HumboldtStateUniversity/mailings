Dear HSU students:

We've been busy all summer preparing the campus for you, and we are glad to have you here! To support your success, we are offering a number of activities during the first few weeks of the semester, designed to connect you with campus services and programs. You can see the entire schedule at humboldt.edu/welcomehome. We encourage you to participate in as many of them as you can – they will help you be successful at Humboldt.

If you attended HOP this summer, you have already heard me talk about the three keys to your success:

  1. Go to class – every day, every week. It's simple, logical, and it works!
  2. Find one program, organization, or activity that you're interested in and get involved in it. It has been proven time and time again: Involvement = Success
  3. If you need help, ask for it – in the classroom, in the residence halls, wherever and whenever you need it. We have the resources dedicated to your success, so please use them when you need help!

I will see you at the free Welcome BBQ on the UC Quad on Monday! And if there is ever anything you need from me, please let me know. I hope you have a great semester!

Welcome home!

Peg Blake
, Enrollment Management and Student Affairs
