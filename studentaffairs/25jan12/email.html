Dear Campus Colleagues:

Nominations are now being accepted for Humboldt State University's 2011-12 Outstanding Student Awards. These awards are given annually in recognition of the outstanding accomplishments of our students.

There are eight categories of academic awards as well as awards for contributions to clubs, programs, community service, activism and athletics. There will be an award winner from each category. Additionally, two Outstanding Students of the Year are selected from among the top candidates.

Award descriptions, criteria, and the online nomination process are available on the forms website at humboldt.edu/forms/outstandingstudents. Your department also received an announcement of these awards in campus mail last week.

This year's award ceremony is scheduled for Thursday, April 12, from 3:00 – 5:00 p.m. in the Kate Buchanan Room. Be sure to mark your calendar now. Nominators and nominees will receive a formal invitation to the awards ceremony.

Please nominate one of your outstanding students and plan to join us at this event!

Sincerely,

Peg L. Blake, Ph.D.
Vice President, Enrollment Management & Student Affairs
