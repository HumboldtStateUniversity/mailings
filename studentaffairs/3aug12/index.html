Faculty and Staff:

HOP is just around the corner and it will be the biggest ever. This year, significant changes were made to the HOP program and all 1300 incoming freshmen will be attending HOP, Wednesday, August 15-Friday, August 17, 2012. Their family members and guests will also be on-campus and participating in the Family & Guest Program.

Orientation is the first time that many students and families have set foot on our campus. It is our opportunity to ensure that their HSU experience begins in a manner that will set them up for success. Please join me in welcoming the newest members of the HSU community when you see them and help them to feel confident in their choice of Humboldt State. Be prepared to assist and support them if they find their way to your offices, or to share information with them if you see them across campus.

Important Dates:

  • Monday, August 13: expect to see new students and families exploring campus
  • Tuesday, August 14: official move-in day for the residence halls. JGC parking lot and Sunset Lane will be closed to staff/faculty parking. Some restrictions in place at G11.
  • Wednesday, August 15: HOP begins at 8am in the UC Quad
  • Friday, August 17: HOP ends at 5pm
  • Monday, August 20: HSU Classes begin
  • Monday, August 20-September 1: Welcome Home to Humboldt. Two weeks of activities open to new and returning students. To learn more, visit humboldt.edu/welcomehome

Please note that due to the number of people expected at HOP, parking may be extremely limited on campus. You are encouraged to use public transportation and carpooling options whenever possible during this week.

Thanks for your support of our new students!

Peg L. Blake, Ph.D.
Vice President, Enrollment Management & Student Affairs
