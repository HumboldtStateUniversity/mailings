Dear HSU Students:

On Sunday, Sept. 2, a 20-year-old Cal Poly San Luis Obispo student named Brett Olson went missing during the Labor Day weekend float down the Sacramento River near Chico. There are currently search and rescue teams all over the area.

We believe that there are students at HSU who either went to the float, or who know people who live near the Chico area. We really need to spread the word as quickly as possible and try to find him.

A current update on the search can be found at: http://www.orovillemr.com/news/ci_21472867/11-m-update-search-continues-brett-olson-tuber

This is the original article that was posted about his disappearance: http://www.chicoer.com/breakingnews/ci_21465843/authorities-searching-student-missing-since-labor-day-float

Physical Description: 6 foot, brown hair, brown eyes

We are hoping that someone has seen him, or could help pass on the news until he is found. Anyone who has information that may be helpful should call the Glenn County Sheriff's Office at (530) 934-6431.

Sincerely,

Peg L. Blake, Ph.D.
Vice President, Enrollment Management & Student Affairs
