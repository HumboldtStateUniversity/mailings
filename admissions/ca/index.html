It's getting to be that time...

Soon you'll be hearing from a bunch of universities — but none of them are quite like Humboldt State University in Arcata, California.

Why Humboldt?

Small classes and real experiences. Passion for the environment. And a great education you can actually afford.

At Humboldt State, you won't spend four years watching your professor from the back of a lecture hall. You'll know your professors. You'll know your classmates. You'll be involved.

Care about the planet? Sustainability is more than just a slogan at HSU — it's something we really care about. New buildings on campus? Green-designed. Paper in the printers? 100% recycled. Plus, our front yard is miles of beaches and our back yard is a towering redwood forest.
