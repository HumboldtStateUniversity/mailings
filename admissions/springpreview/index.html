Dear Spring Preview Attendee,

Spring Preview is almost here and we cannot wait to welcome you and your guests to Humboldt State University this Friday, April 17 and Saturday, April 18. We are delighted to have the opportunity to introduce you to our amazing campus community.

Because we consider you a very important guest, we are glad to provide complimentary parking at the event. Please park in any HSU general or staff parking lot or at a University parking meter, with the exception of parking meters marked 'City of Arcata.' After parking, just follow the event signs to the Admissions Center for check-in. (Parking will be busy on Friday, so please plan to arrive early.)

Currently, HSU is undergoing some construction and Harpst Street (where the Admissions Center is located) is temporarily closed. Our recommendation is to follow the detour signs as you exit Hwy 101and use the ‘14th and B Street’ entrance. To see a printable campus map, please visit: www.humboldt.edu/springpreview/maps.html

To find printable event schedules, finalized 'Go to Class' options and the latest event information, please visit: www.humboldt.edu/admissions/springpreview

We wish you safe travels and will see you at Spring Preview 2009!

Stacie Lyans
Spring Preview Coordinator

If your travel plans change or you have further questions, please contact Stacie Lyans at 707.826.6256 or stacie.lyans@humboldt.edu.
