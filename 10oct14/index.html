You are invited to a student forum from 11:50 a.m. to 12:50 p.m., Thursday, Oct. 16, to share thoughts about the STEM Center for Academic Excellence.

The one-hour forum will be held at the Great Hall of the College Creek Apartments. HSU President Lisa Rossbacher and other campus leaders will be participating.

The Thursday forum is intended as a time to come together as a community to share perspectives and concerns regarding the future direction of the STEM Center.

The STEM Center for Academic Excellence supports underrepresented students majoring in science, technology, engineering, and math fields. It includes a number of programs, including INRSEP, which is focused on supporting Native American students in the sciences.

HSU remains committed to continuing and strengthening these programs. An interim director of the STEM Center will soon be appointed and there will be a national search for a permanent director. Students will be included in the search process for that position.
