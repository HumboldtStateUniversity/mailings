Notification from Humboldt State University

May 12, 2015

Police Urge Caution with Individual

University Police are asking the HSU community to be cautious of Jonathan Marcus Werdal, who is the subject of a restraining order. Police have confirmed that Werdal has been on campus attempting to contact the protected person, who is an HSU student.

Werdal is a 24-year-old white, male, 6' 1", 180 lbs., with blond hair and blue eyes. He has been known to wear military fatigues.

The University Police have issued a flier, available at the bottom of this message, which has a picture and physical description of Werdal. If you see him on campus, avoid contact and notify University Police at (707) 826-5555.
