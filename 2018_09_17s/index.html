A Message from Humboldt State University

September 17, 2018

HSU Releases Annual Campus Security Report

Dear Students,

Pursuant to the Jeanne Clery Disclosure of Campus Security Policy and Campus Crime Statistics Act, the 2018 Annual Security Report (ASR) is now available for viewing at police.humboldt.edu/clery-act-report. The ASR contains the current security and safety-related policy statements, emergency preparedness and evacuation information, crime prevention and sexual assault prevention information, and drug and alcohol prevention programming. The ASR also contains statistics of Clery Act crimes for Humboldt State University for 2015 - 2017. A paper copy of the ASR is available upon request by contacting the Office of the Clery Director located at 1 Harpst Street, Arcata (Student Business Services Rm 345)

In 2017, Humboldt State University, along with other institutions, was audited by the California State Auditors’ office. As a result of the audit’s findings and recommendations, two corrections were made to the 2016 statistics. These changes are reflected in the 2018 ASR. They are identified by an asterisk with explanations of the changes. We have posted the ASRs from 2016, 2017 and 2018 for review.
