A Message from Humboldt State University

January 11, 2021

2021 Spring Welcome Reminder

Please mark your calendar and plan to tune in for the Virtual 2021 Spring Welcome which will take place on Thursday, January 14 from 8:30-10 a.m.

This will be an opportunity to learn about important campus updates and exciting initiatives that are taking place at HSU!

President Jackson will start his Spring Welcome Address at 8:30, and immediately following (around 9:15 a.m.) there will be concurrent breakout sessions with the VPs from each division giving updates about the topics below. You will need to register in advance to attend a session. They will also be recorded and available to view after the Spring Welcome.

The links to Spring Welcome activities will be accessible on the President’s Office website the morning of the event. To register for a breakout session, click on the Zoom link associated with it below.

Time permitting, presenters will be able to address questions about the below topics and others. Submit your questions in advance using this Google form.
