Open Forums: Help Shape HSU’s Vision and Goals

Humboldt State’s Strategic Plan is a blueprint of HSU’s vision and goals for the next five years. It’s a massive undertaking that hinges on ideas from many diverse voices — including yours.
Starting next week, join us for a series of virtual “Future Forward Forums.” This is a chance for members of the HSU and local community to provide feedback on key guiding questions for our themes. This feedback coupled with data will be a valuable resource in helping the working group shape the vision and strategic goals for each theme of the Strategic Plan.
Your input is critical for a Strategic Plan that’s not only bold, but also takes into consideration a range of perspectives. So please spread the word and encourage students, staff, and faculty to participate! If you’re unable to attend the forums, you can use this form to submit your comments and ideas.
Thank you for helping to shape the future of HSU.

Community Collaboration & Shared Success
The Community Collaboration & Shared Success working group is working to ensure that we build a supportive community for students beyond HSU, and that the work of HSU, from teaching to service to research, contributes to vibrant, inclusive, and sustainable communities on the North Coast.

Employee Engagement & Success
The Employee Engagement & Success working group is ensuring all HSU employees—staff, faculty, administration and student employees—have what they need to be involved in, enthusiastic about, and committed to their work and to HSU. Their success is supported throughout their experience with HSU, from recruitment through departure. Join us to lend your voice to this timely and important conversation.

Future Proofing HSU
The Future Proofing HSU working group is examining how to create the type of university that can adapt and thrive in the future and respond to internal and external challenges and opportunities. With students at the center, we look to create the type of environment that provides the educational experience that students want and need, now and in the future.
