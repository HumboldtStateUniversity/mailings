2021-2026 Strategic Plan Phase 1 Draft: Last Day to Comment

Dear Campus Community,
Welcome to the 2021 Spring semester and happy new year. Thank you all who have already posted feedback on the 2021-2026 Strategic Plan Phase 1 document that was presented to the University Senate and shared with campus on December 15. We want to remind everyone that the document is still open for feedback until 5 p.m. on Friday, January 15.
Nearly 100 students, faculty, and staff members rolled up their sleeves and helped create this plan. More than 600 students, faculty, staff, community members, and alumni also participated in public forums, which added additional value to the document.
A number of the comments on this draft indicate that the plan is missing measurable outcomes and specific strategies and tactics. Those critical elements will be the focus of “Phase II." Beginning in February, division leaders will work within their units, engaging in an inclusive planning process to determine the tactics needed to meet HSU’s strategic goals defined in Phase 1.
Go to strategicplan.humboldt.edu for details on the Strategic Plan timeline, themes, working groups, and more.
