Eleven ‘Jacks Earn All-CCAA Honors
Eleven Humboldt State Lumberjacks earned All-California Collegiate Athletic Association (CCAA) honors this past fall.  The men’s soccer team had six all-conference honorees while women’s cross country had two, volleyball had two and women’s soccer had one.
The six honorees for the men’s soccer team were the most since the ‘Jacks since they joined the CCAA in 2006.  Marco Silveira was a First Team selection with Dylan Tovani and Adrian Gallardo being a Second Team honoree.  Three ‘Jacks were Honorable Mention selections in Christian Gonzalez, Devin Hauenstein and Motoki Sato. For the complete release, on the men’s soccer All-CCAA honorees, click here.
Over in the cross country course, both Saedy Williamson and Rosa Granados earned All-CCAA honors by placing in the top ten at the CCAA Championships.  Williamson finished in eighth place while Granados finished in tenth place. The duo of Carla Crespo and Lindsey McLaughlin earned All-CCAA Honorable Mention honors for HSU’s volleyball team.  For the release on Crespo and McLaughlin, click here.  Over on the pitch, senior Claire Bareilles was named to the All-CCAA Women’s Soccer Honorable Mention Team.  For the release on Bareilles being named to the All-CCAA Team, click here.

More Post-Season Honors for Lumberjack Men’s Soccer
Besides the six ‘Jacks earned All-CCAA honors, three HSU men’s soccer players earned regional post-season honors.  Marco Silveira was selected to the D2CCA All-West Region Second Team as voted on by the region’s sports information directors.  Silveira was named to the United Soccer Coaches All-West Region First Team while Dylan Tovani and Adrian Gallardo were Second Team selections. Iman and Edwards Named CCAA Player of the Week

The due of junior transfers Julia Iman and Jaden Edwards have been named the CCAA Players of the Week the first two weeks in December.  Iman received this honor for the week of November 29-December 5 for her performance in HSU’s victories over SF State and Cal State Monterey Bay.  Edwards was named the CCAA Player of the Week for his effort in the Lumberjacks’ victories over Simpson and Stanislaus State for the week of December 6-12.  

Heading into their games at Chico State and Sonoma State (Dec. 18 and 20), the Lumberjack women are 3-4 overall and 2-1 in the CCAA while HSU’s men are 7-2 overall and 1-2 in the CCAA.  For updated information on the Lumberjack women, click here.
