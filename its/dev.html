May 2013

TO: HSU Employees Enrolled in Health Benefits

This to update you on the CalPERS Dependent Eligibility Verification Project (DEV) that begins on July 1, 2013.

In a previous email we discussed the scope of the project--CalPERS will be conducting an audit of all current health plan participants to ensure that only eligible dependents are receiving health plan benefits. We also mentioned that there is currently an amnesty period occurring through June 30, 2013, during which time employees may remove ineligible dependents prior to the end of the amnesty period with no financial consequences.

There are two points we wish to underscore regarding new information from CalPERS:

  • CalPERS has secured a third party vendor, HMS, who will begin contacting employees in July 2013 and requiring documentation of eligible dependent status. This documentation must be provided by the employee directly to the vendor. Campuses cannot initiate the verifications, and will not be allowed to provide copies of verifications to employees, even if on file in HR. We apologize for any inconvenience; please know this project has been initiated by CalPERS to stem rising health care costs which in turn generate higher premiums for employees.

  • Employees who have enrolled a child for whom the employee has assumed a parent-child relationship and is economically dependent through certification, must provide one of the following as documentation for eligibility: A copy of the front page of the employee’s 2012 federal tax return confirming the dependent OR a copy of the court order naming the employee or their spouse as the child’s legal guardian OR day care receipts or school records which indicate the child resides at the employee’s current mailing address, if applicable.

All communications from CalPERS and HMS should be considered time-sensitive and it is important that you open your mail in a timely manner. Please keep your address up to date with Human Resources throughout the duration of this project to ensure that you do not miss any important communications from CalPERS and HMS.  Please use the Employee Action Request - EAR form to update your address.
